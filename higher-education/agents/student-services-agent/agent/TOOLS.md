# Tools

## Housing Management

- **StarRez** — check room assignment (building, room number, roommate), submit maintenance requests, view housing contract terms, check move-in/move-out dates, request room change or reassignment
- **Symplicity Housing** — housing application status, lottery number, waitlist position, roommate matching preferences

## Dining Services

- **Transact / CBORD GET** — check meal plan balance and remaining swipes, view dining location hours and menus, add Dining Dollars, locate campus food pantry resources

## Health & Wellness

- **Medicat (student health EHR)** — view immunization requirements and submission status, book health center appointments, check lab results with student's consent
- **TimelyCare / Uwill** — connect students to telehealth mental health sessions, check session availability, initiate referral to on-campus counseling
- **Counseling appointment portal** — schedule initial consultation, view counseling center hours and emergency contacts, display crisis line numbers prominently

## Disability Services

- **AIM (Accessible Information Management) / Accommodate** — view registered accommodations, submit accommodation request for a course, notify instructor of accommodation letter status

## Campus Life & Events

- **Campus Labs Engage / Presence** — search registered student organizations (name, category, meeting schedule, contact), browse upcoming campus events (date, location, RSVP), check involvement transcript

## Transportation

- **Campus shuttle tracker** — real-time shuttle location and estimated arrival, route maps, service schedule
- **Parking portal** — permit purchase, citation payment, appeal submission

## Data Sources

### Housing

- **StarRez / Symplicity Housing** — room assignment (building, floor, room type, roommate name if consented), housing contract (term, meal plan tier, move-in date), maintenance request history, roommate preference form responses, waitlist status

### Dining

- **Transact / CBORD GET** — meal plan type and remaining balance, Dining Dollar balance, swipe history (anonymized), dining location hours, weekly menu data, allergen information

### Health & Wellness

- **Medicat** — immunization records (required vaccines, submission status, exemption filings), scheduled appointments, referral notes (access limited to student's own record and only with session authentication)
- **TimelyCare / Uwill** — telehealth session availability, provider types, session history (student-consented access only)
- **Counseling center** — appointment availability, provider bios, crisis resources, group counseling schedule

### Disability Services

- **AIM / Accommodate** — registered accommodations (testing, note-taking, housing, dietary, physical access), accommodation letter generation status, instructor notification log

### Student Life

- **Campus Labs Engage / Presence** — student organization directory (name, description, category, meeting schedule, membership count, officer contact), campus events calendar (title, date, time, location, host organization, RSVP count, virtual vs. in-person), student involvement transcript

### Safety & Emergency

- **Campus safety** — emergency procedures (building evacuation plans, shelter-in-place protocols), blue light phone locations, Safe Walk program hours and contact, Title IX reporting contacts
