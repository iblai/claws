# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for sales enablement and revenue operations.

## CRM & Pipeline

- **Salesforce Sales Cloud** -- accounts (account ID, name, industry, annual revenue, employee count, billing address, owner, tier, parent account), opportunities (opportunity ID, name, stage, amount, close date, probability, owner, next steps, competitor, source), pipeline stages (stage name, probability, duration, conversion rate, weighted amount, aging), activity history (task/event type, subject, date, related contact, related opportunity, outcome, duration, notes), forecasts (forecast category, amount, best case, commit, pipeline, quota, attainment), reports and dashboards (report ID, type, filters, groupings, columns, chart type, schedule, recipients)
- **HubSpot Sales Hub** -- deals (deal ID, deal name, stage, amount, close date, owner, pipeline, associated contacts, deal type, source), contacts (contact ID, name, email, phone, company, lifecycle stage, lead status, last activity date, lead score), email tracking (email ID, recipient, open count, click count, reply, timestamp, template, sequence), meeting scheduling (meeting link, availability, booked meetings, meeting type, duration, outcome), sequences (sequence ID, steps, enrollment count, email opens, replies, meetings booked, conversion rate), pipeline management (pipeline ID, stages, deal count per stage, weighted value, velocity, win rate)
- **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Sales** -- opportunity management (opportunity ID, topic, estimated revenue, close date, stage, probability, owner, budget amount, decision maker, timeline), relationship analytics (interaction history, engagement score, relationship health, key contacts, communication frequency, next best action), pipeline forecasting (forecast model, quota, committed, best case, pipeline, won, lost, accuracy)
- **Pipedrive** -- deal management (deal ID, title, value, stage, probability, expected close, owner, organization, contact person, lost reason), activity tracking (activity type, due date, done, duration, deal, contact, note, outcome), pipeline visualization (pipeline, stages, deal count, total value, conversion rate, average deal age, rotting deals), automations (automation ID, trigger, conditions, actions, execution count, conversion impact)

## Sales Engagement

- **Outreach** -- email sequences (sequence ID, name, steps, enrolled prospects, active count, reply rate, meeting rate, opt-out rate), call tasks (task ID, prospect, phone, disposition, duration, recording URL, outcome, notes), meeting scheduling (meeting type, availability, booked, rescheduled, no-show rate, conversion), A/B testing (test ID, variant A/B, metric, sample size, winner, confidence, improvement), rep activity analytics (rep, emails sent, calls made, meetings booked, pipeline generated, response rate, efficiency score)
- **SalesLoft (now Salesloft)** -- cadences (cadence ID, name, steps, person count, step completion rate, reply rate, positive reply rate, meeting rate), call recording (call ID, rep, prospect, duration, talk ratio, keywords, sentiment, coaching notes), email tracking (email ID, template, open rate, click rate, reply rate, bounce rate, opt-out rate), pipeline generation analytics (cadence attribution, pipeline created, revenue influenced, conversion rate by cadence, time-to-opportunity)
- **Apollo.io** -- prospecting (search filters, contact results, enrichment data, verified emails, direct dials, company info), email sequences (sequence ID, steps, prospects, delivery rate, open rate, reply rate, interested rate), contact enrichment (name, title, company, email, phone, LinkedIn URL, technologies, funding, employee count), intent signals (company, topic, signal strength, signal source, trending keywords, timing)
- **Gong** -- conversation intelligence (call ID, participants, duration, talk-to-listen ratio, longest monologue, patience score, filler words, question count), call recording analysis (topics discussed, competitor mentions, pricing discussion, next steps, objections raised, sentiment shifts), deal analytics (deal ID, engagement score, risk signals, activity gap, stakeholder coverage, buying signals, momentum), coaching insights (rep, talk ratio trend, question frequency, topic coverage, winning behaviors, improvement areas)

## Content & Enablement

- **Seismic** -- sales content management (content ID, title, type, format, tags, owner, version, publish date, expiration, permissions), content analytics (content ID, views, shares, downloads, time spent, completion rate, influence on deals, revenue attribution), personalization (template ID, dynamic fields, merge tags, conditional content, audience rules, usage), training modules (lesson ID, topic, format, required/optional, completion rate, assessment score, certification)
- **Highspot** -- content management (content ID, title, type, spot, pitch, analytics, owner, last updated, permissions, compliance status), sales plays (play ID, name, scenario, content, talk track, objection handling, competitive positioning, success metrics), buyer engagement analytics (pitch ID, recipient, views, time spent, shares, forwarded, content interactions, engagement score), training and coaching (module ID, topic, format, completion, practice exercises, manager scoring, certification)
- **Showpad** -- content management (content ID, title, format, collection, tags, expiration, compliance, usage rights), seller effectiveness (rep, content usage, sharing activity, buyer engagement, pipeline correlation, win rate correlation), buyer engagement (share ID, recipient, content viewed, time spent, pages viewed, forwarded, return visits), coaching (coaching activity, rubric, scored sessions, feedback, improvement tracking, certification status)
- **Mindtickle** -- sales readiness (readiness index, skill proficiency, knowledge scores, certification status, practice completion, ramp metrics), onboarding (program ID, new hire, milestones, course completion, assessment scores, ramp time, quota attainment at 90 days), certification (cert ID, topic, passing criteria, attempts, score, expiration, renewal requirements), role-play practice (scenario ID, submission, AI scoring, manager feedback, competency rating, improvement over time)

## Competitive Intelligence

- **Klue** -- competitive intelligence (competitor ID, company name, products, positioning, strengths, weaknesses, recent news, market share), battle cards (card ID, competitor, scenario, talk track, objection handling, proof points, win themes, last updated), win/loss analysis (deal ID, outcome, competitor, decision factors, buyer feedback, loss reasons, win themes, patterns), competitive alerts (alert ID, competitor, change type, source, summary, impact assessment, recommended action, date)
- **Crayon** -- competitive monitoring (competitor, tracked signals, news, product changes, pricing changes, messaging updates, hiring trends, review sentiment), market intelligence (market segment, trends, emerging players, technology shifts, regulatory changes), competitive alerts (alert type, competitor, signal, source URL, date, relevance score, digest frequency, action items)
- **Kompyte (Semrush)** -- competitor tracking (competitor, website changes, SEO metrics, ad campaigns, social activity, content published, technology stack changes), automated battle cards (competitor, auto-generated insights, positioning, pricing, features, strengths, weaknesses, refresh frequency), win rate analysis (competitor, deals won, deals lost, win rate, average deal size, common objections, winning strategies)

## Revenue Intelligence & Analytics

- **Clari** -- revenue forecasting (forecast category, amount, commit, best case, upside, pipeline, AI prediction, confidence level, variance to quota), pipeline analytics (pipeline coverage, stage distribution, velocity, conversion rates, aging, pipeline creation trend), deal inspection (deal ID, health score, engagement level, risk flags, activity gaps, multithreading score, next steps, CRM accuracy), CRM data quality (field completeness, data freshness, activity capture rate, contact coverage, opportunity hygiene score)
- **Gong Revenue Intelligence** -- deal analytics (deal ID, engagement score, activity timeline, stakeholder map, risk signals, buying stage, win probability), conversation trends (topic frequency, competitor mentions, pricing discussions, feature requests, objection patterns, sentiment over time), forecasting (AI forecast, manual forecast, variance, accuracy history, deal movement, commit confidence), risk signals (deal ID, risk type, days without contact, missing stakeholders, competitor activity, budget concerns, delayed timeline)
- **InsightSquared** -- revenue analytics (ARR, MRR, growth rate, churn, expansion, net retention, cohort analysis), forecasting (forecast model, predicted close, confidence interval, scenario analysis, pipeline weighted), pipeline management (pipeline created, pipeline converted, velocity, stage duration, win rate by segment, loss reasons), activity capture (emails, calls, meetings, auto-logged, manual, CRM sync status, coverage)
- **People.ai** -- activity capture (email, calendar, call, contact, account, opportunity, auto-association, timestamp, duration, outcome), contact mapping (account, contacts, roles, engagement level, last activity, relationship strength, buying committee coverage), revenue intelligence (account engagement score, multi-threading score, buyer engagement trend, pipeline risk, coaching recommendation)

## Proposal & Contract

- **PandaDoc** -- proposal creation (document ID, template, content blocks, pricing table, variables, recipients, created date, status), electronic signatures (signature ID, signer, sign date, IP address, authentication, status, audit trail), content library (template ID, category, content blocks, merge fields, usage count, last updated, version), deal rooms (room ID, deal, shared documents, viewer activity, time spent, questions, engagement timeline)
- **Conga (Apttus)** -- CPQ configure-price-quote (quote ID, product configuration, pricing rules, discount approval, terms, output document, version), contract lifecycle management (contract ID, type, parties, effective date, expiration, renewal terms, obligations, amendments, compliance status), document generation (template, merge fields, data source, output format, approval workflow, distribution, audit trail)
- **DocuSign CLM** -- contract management (contract ID, type, status, parties, key dates, obligations, renewal terms, amendment history, repository location), electronic signatures (envelope ID, recipients, routing order, status, signed date, IP, authentication, certificate of completion), workflow automation (workflow ID, trigger, routing rules, approval chain, parallel/serial routing, reminders, expiration, delegation)

## Data Enrichment

- **ZoomInfo** -- company data (company name, domain, industry, revenue, employee count, headquarters, funding, technologies, org chart, subsidiaries), contact data (name, title, seniority, department, email, direct dial, mobile, LinkedIn, job function, location), intent signals (company, topic, signal score, signal source, date range, trending up/down), technographics (company, technology category, product, install date, spend estimate, contract renewal)
- **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** -- lead discovery (search filters, lead results, title, company, location, connections, shared interests, activity), account mapping (company, employees, decision makers, org chart, recent hires, headcount trends, department growth), InMail (message ID, recipient, template, open rate, reply rate, accepted rate), relationship insights (connection degree, shared connections, recent activity, engagement, profile views, common interests)
- **Clearbit (now HubSpot)** -- company enrichment (domain, company name, industry, sector, employee range, revenue range, location, tech stack, social profiles, description), lead scoring data (firmographic score, behavioral score, fit score, intent score, total score, grade), technographic data (technology category, product, install date, spend, first seen, last seen)
- **6sense** -- account-based intent data (account, buying stage, intent topics, intent score, trending keywords, research activity), buying stage prediction (account, predicted stage, confidence, stage progression, time in stage, likely timeline), audience creation (segment name, account criteria, intent criteria, persona criteria, account count, contact count, activation channels)
