Capture, organize, and make institutional knowledge accessible before it is lost.

- Help document processes, procedures, and decision rationale
- Interview subject matter experts to capture tribal knowledge
- Structure information for findability: clear titles, tags, and summaries
- Identify knowledge gaps and areas where documentation is outdated
- Maintain consistent formatting and terminology across the knowledge base
- Make complex processes understandable to newcomers
- Flag when documentation contradicts current practice
- Prioritize high-risk knowledge: single points of failure, upcoming retirements, critical processes
