# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for HR support and employee services.

## Human Resource Information Systems (HRIS)

- **Workday HCM** -- employee records (employee ID, legal name, preferred name, date of birth, SSN last 4, hire date, employment status, worker type), job history (position ID, job title, department, cost center, location, effective date, reason for change, manager), compensation (base salary, pay frequency, currency, bonus target, stock grants, compensation grade, last increase date/percentage), benefits enrollment (medical plan, dental plan, vision plan, life insurance, FSA/HSA elections, dependent coverage, beneficiary designations), org structure (supervisory org, management chain, dotted-line reports, cost center hierarchy), leave balances (PTO accrued, PTO used, PTO available, sick leave, floating holidays, carryover balance)
- **SAP SuccessFactors** -- employee central (person ID, employment record, personal info, job info, compensation info, pay component details), benefits administration (benefit programs, plan options, enrollment status, coverage amounts, contribution rates, qualifying events), time off (leave type, balance, accrual rate, request history, approval chain, policy), performance and goals (goal plan, review form, rating scale, final rating, calibration result, development plan), employee self-service (address changes, bank account updates, tax withholding changes, benefit changes, document requests)
- **Oracle HCM Cloud** -- core HR (person ID, person type, assignment, position, grade, step, location, business unit), absence management (absence type, balance, entitlement, accrual plan, absence request, approval status), workforce compensation (compensation plan, element, amount, currency, effective date, approval, budget impact), help desk (case ID, category, description, priority, assigned agent, SLA, status, resolution)
- **BambooHR** -- employee directory (employee ID, name, photo, title, department, division, location, phone, email, work phone, reports-to), time-off tracking (PTO type, accrual rate, current balance, pending requests, approved history, policy name), document storage (document category, file name, upload date, expiration, acknowledgment status, access permissions), performance management (review cycle, self-assessment, manager assessment, peer feedback, overall rating, goals)
- **ADP Workforce Now** -- payroll data (employee ID, pay period, gross pay, deductions, taxes, net pay, YTD totals, direct deposit), benefits administration (plan type, plan name, coverage level, employee contribution, employer contribution, effective date, status), time and attendance (clock-in/out, hours worked, overtime hours, shift differential, attendance points, exceptions), tax services (federal filing status, state withholdings, local taxes, W-2 data, W-4 elections, tax jurisdiction)
- **UKG Pro (Kronos)** -- workforce management (employee ID, schedule, shift pattern, labor allocation, overtime eligibility, seniority), payroll processing (pay group, pay frequency, earnings codes, deduction codes, garnishments, tax elections, check/direct deposit), HR service delivery (case management, knowledge articles, SLA tracking, employee portal, chatbot interactions), scheduling (schedule template, shift assignments, open shifts, swap requests, availability, forecasting)

## Benefits Administration

- **Benefitfocus** -- benefits enrollment (plan year, eligible plans, elected plans, coverage tier, annual cost, employer contribution, employee premium), plan comparison (plan name, type, deductible, out-of-pocket max, copays, coinsurance, network, prescription tiers), life event changes (event type, event date, impacted plans, enrollment window, required documentation), dependent management (dependent name, relationship, date of birth, SSN, eligibility, coverage elections)
- **bswift** -- benefits administration (enrollment status, plan elections, contribution amounts, payroll deduction schedule, effective dates), enrollment processing (enrollment type, window dates, eligible employees, completion rate, pending actions), billing (carrier invoices, premium reconciliation, variance reports, payment status), COBRA management (qualifying event, election period, coverage continuation, premium amount, payment history, termination date)
- **Employee Navigator** -- benefits enrollment (available plans, employee elections, coverage level, costs, enrollment confirmation), compliance (ACA tracking, 1094-C/1095-C reporting, measurement periods, stability periods, safe harbor status), carrier connections (carrier ID, EDI feed status, enrollment file, discrepancy reports, sync frequency)
- **ADP Benefits** -- benefits enrollment (plan options, enrollment window, elected benefits, waived benefits, evidence of insurability), FSA/HSA management (account type, election amount, contributions YTD, claims submitted, available balance, rollover), COBRA administration (qualifying event type, election deadline, coverage options, premium, payment status, termination reason)

## Payroll & Compensation

- **ADP** -- payroll processing (pay period, pay date, hours, earnings codes, gross pay, tax withholdings, pre-tax deductions, post-tax deductions, net pay, direct deposit allocations), tax filing (federal tax deposits, state tax filings, local tax remittances, quarterly returns, year-end W-2/W-3), wage garnishments (order type, case number, deduction amount, remittance, balance, priority, issuing court), pay statements (pay date, earnings detail, deductions detail, taxes, net pay, YTD summary, PTO balance)
- **Paychex** -- payroll (employee pay data, check date, hours by type, earnings, taxes, deductions, employer taxes, net pay), tax compliance (tax jurisdiction, deposit schedule, filing frequency, quarterly reports, year-end reports), direct deposit (bank routing number, account number, account type, allocation amount/percentage, prenote status), W-2 generation (boxes 1-20, state/local wages, employer EIN, control number, electronic delivery consent)
- **Workday Payroll** -- payroll calculations (earnings, deductions, taxes, garnishments, imputed income, net pay, payslip), pay groups (pay group ID, pay period, run category, payment method, pay calendar, processing schedule), off-cycle payments (payment type, reason, amount, tax treatment, approval, payment date)
- **PayScale / Salary.com** -- compensation benchmarking (job title, market percentile, base salary range, total cash range, geographic adjustment), market pricing (survey sources, effective date, aging factor, match quality, data cut), pay equity analysis (pay gap by demographic, regression analysis, compa-ratio distribution, outliers, remediation recommendations)

## Leave & Time Management

- **Workday Absence** -- leave requests (absence type, start date, end date, daily hours, status, approver, comments), accrual balances (plan name, accrued hours, used hours, available hours, carryover, cap, forfeiture date), leave policies (policy name, eligibility criteria, accrual rate, max balance, carryover rules, waiting period), return-to-work tracking (expected return date, actual return date, fitness-for-duty clearance, accommodation needs, modified duty)
- **Kronos / UKG Dimensions** -- time tracking (punch timestamp, punch type, location, source device, attestation, rounding rule applied), scheduling (schedule period, assigned shifts, open shifts, swap requests, overtime forecast, staffing level), overtime management (overtime hours, overtime type, authorization, rate multiplier, cost allocation), compliance (meal/rest break compliance, minor labor rules, predictive scheduling, daily/weekly overtime thresholds)
- **AbsenceSoft** -- leave of absence management (leave type, FMLA/ADA/state leave, start date, end date, intermittent schedule, certification status, employer designation), ADA accommodations (accommodation request, interactive process notes, accommodation type, job essential functions, provider documentation, outcome), FMLA tracking (eligibility, hours worked, 12-month period, leave entitlement, leave used, remaining balance, recertification due), state leave compliance (state, leave type, eligibility requirements, benefit amount, claim filing, documentation)

## Performance & Engagement

- **Lattice** -- performance reviews (review cycle, review type, self-review, manager review, peer feedback, upward feedback, final rating, calibration), goal tracking (goal title, key results, progress, owner, team, status, due date, alignment), 1:1 agendas (meeting date, agenda items, talking points, action items, private notes), engagement surveys (survey name, participation rate, engagement score, dimension scores, benchmark comparison, comments), compensation management (compensation cycle, budget, merit increase, bonus, equity grant, compa-ratio, manager recommendation)
- **Culture Amp** -- employee engagement (survey ID, engagement score, factor scores, driver analysis, benchmark, participation rate, demographic cuts), onboarding surveys (survey timing, new hire sentiment, manager support rating, role clarity, resource satisfaction), exit surveys (departure reason, tenure, engagement history, rehire recommendation, manager rating, improvement themes), action planning (insight area, action description, owner, due date, status, impact measurement)
- **15Five** -- weekly check-ins (check-in date, pulse score, priorities, accomplishments, challenges, manager response), OKRs (objective, key results, confidence level, progress, owner, alignment, status), high-five recognition (sender, recipient, value alignment, message, visibility, date), engagement pulse (pulse question, score, trend, department comparison, alert threshold)
- **Workday Performance** -- performance reviews (review template, review period, competency ratings, goal ratings, overall rating, manager narrative, employee narrative), calibration (calibration pool, proposed ratings, calibrated ratings, distribution curve, justification), talent assessments (potential rating, performance rating, 9-box placement, retention risk, key talent flag)

## Compliance & Policy

- **SHRM** -- HR policy templates (policy area, template version, jurisdiction, last updated, compliance notes, customization guidance), compliance updates (regulation, effective date, impact areas, required actions, jurisdiction), regulatory guidance (topic, applicable laws, employer obligations, employee rights, penalties, best practices)
- **Mineral (formerly ThinkHR)** -- HR compliance library (topic, federal/state applicability, last updated, related forms, summary, full text), live advisor access (question category, response, citations, follow-up resources, consultation notes), employee handbook builder (section, policy text, jurisdiction-specific clauses, version, last reviewed, acknowledgment tracking)
- **ComplianceHR** -- FLSA classification (job title, duties test, salary threshold, classification recommendation, risk assessment, documentation), employment law guidance (topic, jurisdiction, law, employer requirements, exceptions, effective date), ACA compliance (measurement period, stability period, full-time determination, offer of coverage, affordability, reporting)

## Case Management

- **ServiceNow HR Service Delivery** -- HR cases (case ID, employee, category, subcategory, description, priority, assigned agent, status, SLA, resolution, satisfaction rating), knowledge base (article ID, title, category, content, views, helpfulness rating, last updated, owner), employee portal (service catalog, request forms, case status, knowledge search, chatbot interactions), SLA tracking (SLA definition, target response time, target resolution time, actual times, breach status, escalation)
- **Neocase** -- HR case management (case ID, type, employee, inquiry detail, assigned team, status, priority, resolution time, knowledge article linked), employee self-service (portal queries, FAQ access, form submissions, document requests, case creation), process automation (workflow ID, trigger, automated steps, routing rules, notification templates, escalation rules)
- **Dovetail** -- HR help desk (ticket ID, employee, category, inquiry, channel, assigned agent, response time, resolution time, status), case tracking (case lifecycle, status transitions, audit trail, related cases, attachments, notes), knowledge management (article ID, topic, content, usage count, rating, suggested for case type, last reviewed)
