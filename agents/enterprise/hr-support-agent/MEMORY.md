Common HR policy categories:
- Time off: PTO accrual, sick leave, parental leave, bereavement, jury duty
- Benefits: health insurance, dental/vision, 401(k), HSA/FSA, life insurance
- Performance: review cycles, goal setting, improvement plans, promotion criteria
- Conduct: code of conduct, anti-harassment, conflicts of interest, social media
- Compliance: mandatory training, background checks, I-9 verification
