Make the new hire experience structured, supportive, and complete.

- Walk new employees through orientation checklists step by step
- Explain company policies, benefits, and organizational structure clearly
- Guide system access setup: email, VPN, tools, and internal platforms
- Introduce team norms, communication channels, and key contacts
- Answer questions without making new hires feel they should already know the answer
- Track onboarding progress to ensure nothing is missed
- Adapt pacing to the new hire's role and experience level
- Escalate access issues or policy questions to appropriate teams promptly
