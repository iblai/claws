# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for career development and internal mobility.

## HRIS & Talent Management

- **Workday HCM** -- job profiles (job profile ID, title, job family, management level, required skills, required certifications, pay grade range), competency frameworks (competency ID, name, description, proficiency scale, behavioral indicators per level), career paths (source job profile, target job profile, typical transition time, skill gaps, development activities), internal job postings (requisition ID, title, department, location, grade, required skills, hiring manager, application deadline), performance history (review period, rating, calibrated rating, manager comments, goal achievement percentage, development areas), succession planning (position ID, successor candidates, readiness level, development plan, risk of loss, impact of loss)
- **SAP SuccessFactors** -- career development planning (plan ID, worker ID, current role, target role, milestones, timeline, status), talent profiles (worker ID, skills, certifications, education, work experience, career interests, mobility preferences), mentoring programs (program ID, mentee, mentor, objectives, duration, meeting cadence, progress notes), succession management (position, nominees, readiness rating, development actions, bench strength indicator)
- **Oracle HCM Cloud** -- talent review (review meeting ID, participants, talent pool, 9-box placement, action items, calibration notes), career development plans (plan ID, goals, activities, milestones, completion criteria, approval status), goal management (goal ID, type, description, weight, target, actual, status, alignment to org goals), internal mobility records (transfer history, lateral moves, promotions, geographic mobility, functional mobility)
- **Cornerstone OnDemand (Talent)** -- succession planning (plan ID, critical positions, successor pipeline, readiness tiers, risk assessment), development plans (plan ID, competency gaps, learning activities, stretch assignments, mentoring, timeline), competency assessments (assessment ID, competency, self-rating, manager rating, gap score, recommended actions)

## Internal Mobility & Job Matching

- **Gloat** -- internal talent marketplace (opportunity ID, type, required skills, time commitment, department, project description), project matching (project ID, required skills, matched employees, match score, availability window), skill-based opportunity discovery (employee skills, matching opportunities, relevance score, growth potential), career pathing (current role, potential paths, probability scores, skill requirements per path, estimated timelines)
- **Eightfold AI** -- talent intelligence (employee ID, inferred skills, skill confidence score, career trajectory prediction), skill inference (activity-based skills, validated skills, skill adjacencies, proficiency estimates), career path recommendations (current role, recommended paths, success probability, skill gap analysis, peer comparisons), internal mobility matching (open roles, matched candidates, match score, transferable skills, development needs)
- **Phenom** -- internal career site (posted roles, role descriptions, skill requirements, application workflow, employee referrals), AI job matching (employee profile, matched roles, fit score, skill alignment, location/schedule compatibility), employee experience (career site engagement, job alerts, saved searches, application history)
- **Workday Career Hub** -- internal job discovery (open positions, skill match score, location, department, application status), mentoring connections (mentor profile, mentee profile, shared skills, matching rationale, program enrollment), skill development suggestions (current skills, target role skills, recommended learning, estimated development time)

## Skills & Assessment

- **Workday Skills Cloud** -- skill taxonomy (skill ID, name, category, subcategory, related skills, demand trend), skill gap analysis (worker ID, current skills, target role skills, gap severity, priority, recommended interventions), market skill benchmarks (skill, market demand index, salary premium, growth rate, regional variations)
- **Degreed** -- skill ratings (skill name, self-rating, peer ratings, manager rating, evidence count, last assessed), peer validation (validator ID, skill, rating, evidence reviewed, validation date), learning recommendations tied to career goals (target role, skill gaps, recommended content, content source, estimated time)
- **SHL / Korn Ferry Assess** -- behavioral assessments (assessment ID, competency scores, behavioral indicators, percentile ranks, norm group), cognitive assessments (assessment type, score, percentile, problem-solving index, learning agility), leadership potential evaluation (potential rating, readiness factors, derailers, development priorities, acceleration recommendations)
- **Hogan Assessments** -- personality assessment (HPI scales, motives/values/preferences, challenge report, percentile scores), leadership development (strengths, risk factors, values, coaching recommendations), derailment risk (risk factors, risk severity, behavioral indicators, mitigation strategies)

## Performance & Goals

- **Workday Performance** -- goal setting (goal ID, type, description, measure, target, weight, alignment, status), performance reviews (review period, self-assessment, manager assessment, overall rating, calibration group), calibration (calibration session, participants, pre-calibration ratings, post-calibration ratings, distribution), continuous feedback (feedback ID, from, to, type, content, competency tags, date, visibility)
- **Lattice** -- goal tracking (goal ID, title, owner, key results, progress percentage, check-in frequency, status), 1:1 meeting notes (meeting date, manager, employee, agenda items, action items, follow-ups), career growth plans (plan ID, current role, aspirational role, milestones, skills to develop, resources, timeline), engagement surveys (survey ID, response date, engagement score, eNPS, category scores, verbatim comments)
- **15Five** -- continuous performance (check-in ID, week, priorities, accomplishments, challenges, pulse score), OKR tracking (objective ID, key results, progress, confidence level, owner, team alignment), engagement check-ins (check-in date, pulse score, sentiment, manager follow-up status)
- **Culture Amp** -- employee engagement surveys (survey ID, participation rate, engagement score, factor scores, benchmark comparison), performance insights (performance rating distribution, high performer identification, development themes), action planning (action ID, survey insight, owner, status, target date, impact area)

## Mentoring & Networking

- **MentorcliQ** -- mentorship matching (match ID, mentor ID, mentee ID, matching criteria, compatibility score, program type), program management (program ID, program type, enrollment count, active pairs, completion rate, duration), engagement tracking (pair ID, meeting count, meeting frequency, milestone completion, satisfaction ratings)
- **Chronus** -- mentoring programs (program ID, type, mentor pool, mentee pool, matching algorithm, curriculum), ERG management (ERG ID, name, members, leadership, events, goals, budget), coaching programs (coach ID, coachee ID, focus areas, session count, progress notes, outcomes)
- **PeopleGrove** -- alumni and employee networking (network ID, members, connections, interaction count, event attendance), mentorship facilitation (mentor availability, mentee goals, match suggestions, session scheduling, feedback), community building (community ID, topic, members, posts, engagement rate, events)

## Analytics & Reporting

- **Visier** -- workforce analytics (headcount trends, span of control, diversity metrics, workforce composition), attrition risk (risk score, contributing factors, flight risk indicators, retention recommendations), promotion equity (promotion rates by demographic, time-to-promotion, pay equity post-promotion), internal mobility rates (internal fill rate, lateral move rate, promotion rate, cross-functional moves, average tenure at promotion)
- **Tableau / Power BI** -- career path visualization (role transitions, common paths, time at each level, success rates), skill gap dashboards (skill coverage by team, critical skill shortages, development investment, emerging skills), internal hire rate tracking (internal vs external fill rate, time-to-fill, cost-per-hire internal vs external, quality of hire)
