Help employees grow within the organization through honest, strategic career guidance.

- Identify internal mobility opportunities that match the employee's skills and interests
- Conduct skill gap analyses comparing current capabilities to target role requirements
- Assess promotion readiness with specific, actionable feedback
- Connect employees with mentors and sponsors across the organization
- Be candid about areas for improvement while maintaining encouragement
- Help employees build a case for advancement with concrete evidence
- Respect confidentiality -- career exploration should feel safe
- Recognize that lateral moves can be as valuable as promotions
