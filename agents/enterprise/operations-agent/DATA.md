# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for operations management.

## ERP & Operations Management

- **SAP S/4HANA** -- production planning (production order ID, material, quantity, start/end date, routing, BOM, status, yield, scrap rate), materials management (material number, description, plant, storage location, stock quantity, reorder point, MRP type, safety stock, lead time), quality management (inspection lot, inspection plan, results, usage decision, defect codes, quality notifications), plant maintenance (functional location, equipment, maintenance plan, work orders, notification, breakdown hours, MTBF, MTTR)
- **Oracle Cloud ERP** -- supply chain management (supply chain network, demand plans, supply plans, inventory policies, fulfillment rules, ATP), manufacturing (work order ID, item, quantity, routing, operation, resource, status, actual vs planned, variance), procurement (PO number, supplier, items, unit price, quantity, delivery date, receipt status, invoice match, payment), project management (project ID, tasks, schedule, budget, actual cost, earned value, milestones, resource assignments)
- **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Operations** -- production control (production order, BOM version, route version, scheduling, job card, reporting, completion), warehouse management (warehouse, zone, location, item, lot, serial, inventory status, work order, wave, load), asset management (asset ID, asset type, location, criticality, maintenance plan, work orders, downtime log, lifecycle state)
- **Infor CloudSuite** -- industry-specific ERP (industry vertical, modules, configurations, workflows, master data, transaction data, reporting), asset management (asset ID, location, class, status, depreciation, maintenance schedule, warranty, spare parts, cost history)

- **ETQ Reliance** -- quality events (event ID, type, source, description, impact, investigation, root cause, disposition, closure), CAPA management (CAPA ID, trigger event, root cause analysis method, corrective actions, preventive actions, verification, effectiveness review, target dates), document control (document ID, type, version, lifecycle state, review cycle, training requirements, access control), supplier quality (supplier ID, scorecard, audit results, non-conformances, corrective actions, qualification status, risk rating)

- **Greenlight Guru** -- medical device QMS (design history file, risk management file, production record, CAPA file, audit trail), design controls (design input, output, verification, validation, review, transfer, change, traceability matrix), risk management (risk ID, hazard, hazardous situation, harm, severity, probability, risk level, mitigation, residual risk, ISO 14971 compliance), CAPA (CAPA ID, source, nonconformity, root cause, correction, corrective action, effectiveness, regulatory reporting)
