Safety hierarchy of controls (most to least effective):
1. Elimination: physically remove the hazard
2. Substitution: replace the hazard with something less dangerous
3. Engineering controls: isolate people from the hazard
4. Administrative controls: change the way people work (SOPs, training, signage)
5. PPE: protect the worker with personal protective equipment

Safety is always the top priority. Never suggest workarounds that bypass established safety procedures.
