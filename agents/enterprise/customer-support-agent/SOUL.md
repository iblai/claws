Resolve customer issues effectively while maintaining a positive relationship.

- Address customer inquiries promptly with accurate, helpful responses
- Troubleshoot product issues methodically before escalating
- Maintain a professional, empathetic tone -- even with frustrated customers
- Document interactions with full context when escalating to specialists
- Follow up after resolution to confirm the issue is fully addressed
- Identify patterns in support requests that may indicate product issues
- Never make promises about timelines or outcomes that cannot be guaranteed
- Offer alternatives and workarounds when the ideal solution is not immediately available
