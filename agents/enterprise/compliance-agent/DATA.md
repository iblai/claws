# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for corporate compliance and regulatory training.

## Compliance Management

- **SAI360** -- compliance program management (program ID, framework, domains, maturity level, ownership, review cycle), policy management (policy ID, title, version, effective date, owner, review date, approval chain, acknowledgment rate), incident tracking (incident ID, type, severity, reporter, investigation status, root cause, corrective action, closure date), risk assessments (assessment ID, risk domain, inherent risk, controls, residual risk, risk owner, mitigation plan, due date)
- **NAVEX Global** -- ethics and compliance platform (program elements, maturity assessment, benchmarking data, culture survey results), policy management (policy ID, title, category, version, effective date, audience, distribution status, acknowledgment tracking, overdue acknowledgments), incident management (incident ID, source, category, severity, location, investigation owner, status, resolution, days open), disclosures (disclosure ID, type, reporter, conflict description, review status, mitigation, annual certification)
- **LogicGate Risk Cloud** -- compliance workflows (workflow ID, steps, assignees, due dates, status, SLA, completion evidence), risk assessments (risk ID, category, likelihood, impact, inherent score, control effectiveness, residual score, treatment plan), control mapping (control ID, framework, requirement, control description, owner, test frequency, last tested, effectiveness rating), audit management (audit ID, scope, auditor, findings, severity, remediation, due date, status)
- **Resolver** -- compliance management (obligation ID, regulation, requirement, owner, compliance status, evidence, due date), incident tracking (incident ID, type, severity, reported date, location, investigation findings, corrective actions, closure), regulatory change management (change ID, regulation, change description, impact assessment, affected policies, implementation deadline, status), audit scheduling (audit ID, type, scope, auditor, scheduled date, status, findings, follow-up)
- **OneTrust** -- privacy compliance GDPR/CCPA (assessment ID, regulation, data processing activity, legal basis, data subject rights, cross-border transfers, DPIA), data mapping (data element, data category, source system, storage location, retention period, processing purpose, legal basis, third-party sharing), consent management (consent ID, purpose, channel, consent status, collection date, withdrawal date, proof), assessment automation (assessment type, respondent, questions, responses, risk score, remediation tasks, due date)

- **Workday Learning** -- mandatory training assignments (assignment rule, population criteria, course, due date calculation, escalation rules, exception handling), compliance learning campaigns (campaign ID, regulation, target population, content, schedule, reminder cadence, completion percentage, non-compliant list)
- **Litmos** -- compliance course library (course ID, regulation, jurisdiction, topic, duration, format, version, last updated), automated assignments (rule ID, trigger criteria, course, due date, recurrence, notification schedule), completion certificates (cert ID, employee, course, completion date, score, certificate URL, expiration)
- **KnowBe4** -- security awareness training (campaign ID, module, target group, enrollment date, completion date, score, pass/fail), phishing simulation (campaign ID, template, target users, sent count, clicked count, reported count, click rate, failure rate, repeat offenders), compliance modules (module ID, regulation, topic, version, assignment rules, completion tracking, remedial training triggers)
