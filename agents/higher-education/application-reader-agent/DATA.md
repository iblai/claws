# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for application review and evaluation.

## Application Management

- **Technolutions Slate** -- application reader queues (assigned applications, queue depth, priority flags, deadline tracking), reviewer scorecards (rubric dimensions, numeric scores, qualitative comments, overall recommendation), committee workflows (round assignments, committee composition, voting records), decision tracking (admit, deny, waitlist, defer, appeal status, decision date, decision authority), document management (checklists, missing items, supplemental materials, document upload timestamps)
- **Ellucian Banner** -- admissions application records (application ID, term, level, admit type, residency, application date), decision codes (admit, conditional admit, deny, waitlist, withdrawn), admit type classifications (first-time freshman, transfer, readmit, non-degree, international), application fee payment status, student attribute codes (first-generation, legacy, veteran, athlete), checklist items and completion status
- **Common Application (CommonApp)** -- submitted applications: personal information (name, DOB, address, citizenship, language, demographics), education history (current school, GPA, class rank, coursework, honors), testing data (self-reported scores, score-send requests), activities list (type, position, description, hours/week, weeks/year, all-year flag), honors and distinctions, essays (personal statement, supplemental prompts), additional information section, disciplinary history disclosure, fee waiver certification (type, counselor confirmation), FERPA release authorization
- **Coalition Application** -- application data (personal information, family, education, activities, essays), student portfolios (documents, images, videos, reflections uploaded over time), locker submissions (curated portfolio items selected for application), recommender information and submission status, collaboration space records
- **ApplyTexas / SUNY Application** -- state-specific application submissions (personal information, residency determination, academic history, standardized test scores, program/major selection), state-mandated essay prompts, auto-admit eligibility indicators, dual-credit coursework, EOP/special program eligibility data

## Document & Transcript Services

- **Parchment** -- electronic transcript delivery (sending institution, transcript type, delivery date, delivery status), credential evaluation status, transcript data (institution name, dates attended, courses with grades, cumulative GPA, degree conferred, graduation date, class rank if available), document authenticity verification, digital seal validation
- **National Student Clearinghouse** -- enrollment verification (institution FICE/OPEID, enrollment dates, enrollment status, enrollment level), degree verification (degree title, major, date conferred, honors), prior institution history (all colleges attended with dates and enrollment intensity), transfer-out indicators, concurrent enrollment flags
- **Credential Solutions** -- transcript ordering and fulfillment (order status, delivery method, recipient), document delivery tracking (sent date, received date, processing status), transcript hold indicators
- **WES (World Education Services)** -- international credential evaluation reports (course-by-course analysis, US degree equivalency, GPA conversion, credit hour equivalency), institution accreditation verification, grading scale conversion, document authentication status, country-specific education system context
- **ECE (Educational Credential Evaluators)** -- foreign credential assessment (degree equivalency, major/field classification, credit conversion), institution recognition status, grading scale interpretation, professional licensure eligibility evaluation
- **SpanTran** -- international transcript evaluation (course-by-course or document-by-document), US equivalency determination, credit hour and GPA conversion, country education system analysis, authentication and translation services status

## Testing & Score Reporting

- **College Board** -- SAT scores (total score 400-1600, Evidence-Based Reading and Writing 200-800, Math 200-800, test date, percentiles, superscoring eligibility), AP exam results (subject name, score 1-5, exam year, credit recommendation), SAT Subject Test scores (subject, score 200-800, percentile, discontinued after 2021)
- **ACT** -- ACT scores (composite 1-36, English 1-36, Mathematics 1-36, Reading 1-36, Science 1-36, optional Writing 2-12, test date), superscore composite, state-mandated vs. national test administration, ELA score
- **ETS (TOEFL / GRE)** -- TOEFL iBT scores (total 0-120, Reading 0-30, Listening 0-30, Speaking 0-30, Writing 0-30, test date, MyBest scores), GRE scores (Verbal Reasoning 130-170, Quantitative Reasoning 130-170, Analytical Writing 0-6, test date, percentiles), score validity period (5 years), institutional score recipients
- **IELTS** -- IELTS scores (Overall Band Score 0-9, Listening 0-9, Reading 0-9, Writing 0-9, Speaking 0-9, test date), Academic vs. General Training designation, TRF number, test center, validity period (2 years)
- **Duolingo English Test** -- overall score (10-160), subscores (Literacy, Comprehension, Conversation, Production), test date, certified status, video interview recording, writing sample, score validity
- **LSAC (CAS)** -- Credential Assembly Service reports (LSAT score, LSAT percentile, LSAT writing sample, undergraduate GPA, LSAC GPA conversion, all transcript summaries, letters of recommendation), multiple LSAT score history, score band information
- **GMAC** -- GMAT scores (Total 200-800, Quantitative 6-51, Verbal 6-51, Integrated Reasoning 1-8, Analytical Writing 0-6, test date, percentiles), GMAT Focus Edition scores (Total 205-805, Quantitative 60-90, Verbal 60-90, Data Insights 60-90), score validity (5 years), ESR (Enhanced Score Report) data

## Evaluation & Review Tools

- **Kira Talent** -- video assessment submissions (timed video responses, recording metadata, question prompts), written assessment responses (timed written answers), reviewer scoring (rubric-based scores, qualitative comments, comparative rankings), candidate completion status, assessment template configuration
- **InitialView** -- interview recordings (unscripted conversational interview video, interview duration, interviewer notes), writing sample evaluations (timed essay, topic prompt, evaluator comments), character assessment observations, English language proficiency indicators
- **SlideRoom** -- portfolio submissions for arts and design programs (images, video, audio, documents, PDFs), media metadata (file type, dimensions, duration), applicant artist statements, reviewer scoring (rubric criteria, scores, comments), program-specific portfolio requirements and compliance status
- **Interfolio** -- letters of recommendation (recommender name, title, institution, relationship to applicant, letter text, confidentiality waiver status), dossier management (CV, teaching philosophy, research statement, writing samples, diversity statement), document delivery and request tracking

## Analytics & Decision Support

- **EAB Enroll360** -- predictive yield modeling (individual admit yield probability, segment-level yield estimates), financial aid optimization (discount rate scenarios, merit aid impact on yield, net revenue per student estimates), class-shaping analytics (demographic targets, academic profile targets, geographic diversity goals)
- **Rapid Insight Veera** -- predictive analytics for admit likelihood and yield (enrollment probability scores, key predictor variables, confidence intervals), historical admit cohort comparisons, admit-to-enroll conversion modeling, financial aid sensitivity analysis
- **Tableau / Power BI** -- application volume dashboards (applications by date, source, program, geography, demographics), reader progress tracking (reviews completed, pending, average time per review, score distributions), demographic breakdowns (ethnicity, gender, geographic origin, citizenship, first-generation status), admit rate and yield rate trends, waitlist movement analytics
