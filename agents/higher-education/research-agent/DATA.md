# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for research support and scholarly activity.

## Academic Databases & Search

- **PubMed / MEDLINE (NLM)** -- biomedical and life sciences literature (article title, authors, abstract, journal, publication date, DOI, PMID, MeSH terms, publication type, affiliation, full-text links, citation counts, related articles, clinical trial registration numbers)
- **Scopus (Elsevier)** -- multidisciplinary abstract and citation database (article metadata: title, authors, affiliations, abstract, keywords, DOI, ISSN; citation data: citation count, citing documents, h-index, field-weighted citation impact; author profiles: Scopus Author ID, document count, co-author network; institutional profiles: publication output, collaboration maps, subject area strengths)
- **Web of Science (Clarivate)** -- citation indexes: Science Citation Index Expanded, Social Sciences Citation Index, Arts & Humanities Citation Index (article records: title, authors, source, abstract, keywords, cited references, times cited, DOI; journal metrics: Impact Factor, Eigenfactor, Article Influence Score; author records: Web of Science ResearcherID, publication list, citation history)
- **Google Scholar** -- broad academic search (full-text and metadata search across disciplines, citation counts, related articles, citing articles, versions and links), citation tracking (author profiles: publications list, citation counts, h-index, i10-index, citation graph over time), related articles (algorithmically determined related works, co-citation analysis)
- **JSTOR** -- archived journals, books, and primary sources (article full-text, metadata: title, author, journal, volume, issue, pages, stable URL; book chapters; primary source documents; research reports; pamphlets), discipline coverage (humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, mathematics)
- **IEEE Xplore** -- electrical engineering, computer science, electronics literature (conference proceedings, journal articles, standards documents, technical reports; metadata: title, authors, abstract, keywords, DOI, INSPEC accession number, patent citations, standard number)
- **ACM Digital Library** -- computing and information technology research (journal articles, conference proceedings, newsletters, magazines; metadata: title, authors, abstract, ACM classification, DOI, citation count, download count, altmetrics; ACM Computing Reviews, Turing Award lectures)
- **ERIC (Education Resources Information Center)** -- education research and literature (journal articles, reports, conference papers, dissertations; ERIC descriptors, identifiers, publication type, education level, audience, institution, sponsor, ERIC number, full-text availability)
- **PsycINFO / PsycArticles (APA)** -- psychology and behavioral sciences literature (journal articles, book chapters, dissertations; APA thesaurus terms, methodology, population group, age group, test and measures used, grant/sponsorship, clinical trial details)
- **EconLit (AEA)** -- economics literature and working papers (journal articles, working papers, book reviews, dissertations, collective volume articles; JEL classification codes, geographic descriptors, subject descriptors, abstract)
- **arXiv** -- preprints in physics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative biology, statistics (preprint full-text PDF/HTML, metadata: title, authors, abstract, subject classification, submission date, revision history, arXiv ID, DOI if published, comments, journal reference if published)

## Preprint & Open Access

- **bioRxiv / medRxiv** -- biology and medical science preprints (preprint full-text, metadata: title, authors, abstract, subject area, posting date, DOI, revision history, corresponding author, competing interests, supplementary materials, usage metrics: abstract views, PDF downloads, altmetric score)
- **SSRN** -- social science research preprints (full-text papers, metadata: title, authors, abstract, JEL codes, keywords, posted date, revision dates, download count, citation count, SSRN abstract ID, subject networks: law, economics, management, accounting, etc.)
- **PubMed Central (PMC)** -- free full-text archive of biomedical and life sciences literature (full-text XML/HTML/PDF, PMCID, grant support details, reference lists, supplementary data, author manuscripts, NIH public access compliance, open access subset)
- **DOAJ (Directory of Open Access Journals)** -- open access journal directory (journal records: title, ISSN, publisher, country, language, subject, license type: CC-BY, CC-BY-SA, etc.; APC amount, review process type, archiving policy, journal metadata, article-level DOI availability)
- **Unpaywall** -- legal open access article discovery (DOI-based lookup, OA status: gold, green, hybrid, bronze; OA location URL, version: published, accepted, submitted; host type: publisher, repository; license information)

## Citation & Reference Management

- **Zotero** -- reference collection (metadata import from databases and web: title, authors, journal, year, DOI, ISBN, URL, abstract, tags, notes), organization (library structure: collections, sub-collections, tags, saved searches, related items), citation generation (in-document citations, bibliography formatting in 10,000+ styles: APA, MLA, Chicago, Vancouver, etc.), group libraries (shared reference collections, group permissions, collaborative annotation, conflict resolution)
- **Mendeley (Elsevier)** -- reference management (reference library: metadata fields, PDF storage, PDF annotation with highlights and notes), PDF annotation (highlight, sticky note, text annotation, annotation export), academic social network (researcher profile, publication list, follower/following, research interests, discipline, reading statistics)
- **EndNote (Clarivate)** -- reference management (library records: reference types, metadata fields, file attachments, custom fields), bibliography formatting (output styles, manuscript templates, journal-specific formatting, cite-while-you-write for Word), manuscript templates (journal submission templates, formatting automation, reference style switching)
- **RefWorks (ProQuest)** -- cloud-based reference management (reference import from databases, manual entry, duplicate detection), citation formatting (output styles, in-text citations, bibliographies, footnotes, batch formatting), folder organization and sharing

## Grant & Funding

- **Grants.gov** -- federal grant opportunity search (opportunity number, CFDA number, agency, title, description, eligibility criteria, award ceiling/floor, estimated total funding, expected number of awards, application deadline, submission requirements), application submission (application packages, forms: SF-424, budget narrative, project narrative, biosketches; submission receipt, tracking number, status)
- **NSF Award Search** -- National Science Foundation funded project database (award number, title, PI name, institution, program, directorate, division, award amount, start/end dates, abstract, publications, project outcomes, co-PIs, NSF organization code)
- **NIH RePORTER** -- NIH-funded research project database (project number, PI, institution, NIH institute/center, activity code, funding mechanism, fiscal year funding, project dates, abstract, specific aims, publications linked to project, clinical trials, patents), funding trends (spending by institute/center, research category, disease area, geographic distribution)
- **Pivot-RP (ProQuest)** -- funding opportunity discovery (sponsor name, program title, deadline, amount, eligibility: citizenship, career stage, discipline; activity type: research, travel, fellowship, equipment; keyword matching), sponsor profiles (sponsor details, active programs, funding history, contact information), collaborator matching (researcher profiles, expertise keywords, funded projects, institutional affiliation, collaboration interest)
- **SPIN (InfoEd)** -- sponsored program information network (funding opportunities: sponsor, program, deadline, amount, eligibility, keywords, CFDA number, opportunity type), funding search (discipline-based search, keyword matching, saved searches, email alerts for new opportunities)
- **Foundation Directory Online (Candid)** -- private foundation and corporate grant opportunities (foundation name, EIN, total giving, asset size, geographic focus, subject focus, types of support, application procedures, recent grants: recipient, amount, purpose, year)

## Research Administration

- **Cayuse** -- grants management (proposal development: budget building, personnel, subcontracts, cost sharing, compliance certifications; routing and approvals, submission tracking, award setup, account creation), IRB submissions (protocol applications: study title, PI, study type, risk level, participant population, consent procedures, data handling, adverse event reporting; continuing review, amendments, closure reports, approval status, expiration dates), conflict of interest disclosure (annual disclosures, significant financial interests, entity relationships, management plans, review status)
- **InfoEd** -- proposal development (budget templates, personnel effort, cost categories, F&A rate application, sponsor-specific forms), award management (award setup, account numbers, budget allocations, expenditure tracking, no-cost extensions, carryforward requests, closeout), compliance tracking (human subjects, animal subjects, biosafety, export control, conflict of interest; protocol status, approval dates, expiration alerts)
- **Kuali Research** -- research administration suite (proposal development, budget, routing, submission, award management, subaward management), compliance modules (IRB protocols, IACUC protocols, IBC protocols, COI disclosures; protocol lifecycle management, amendment tracking, continuing review tracking), conflict of interest (disclosure forms, financial interests, entity relationships, reviewer assignments, management plan tracking)
- **IACUC / IRB systems (iRIS, Mentor IRB)** -- human subjects protocol management (protocol applications, informed consent documents, recruitment materials, data collection instruments, risk assessment, vulnerable population safeguards, HIPAA considerations, approval certificates, modification requests), animal research protocol management (species, number of animals, procedures, anesthesia/analgesia, veterinary care plans, USDA pain categories, triennial review, annual reports)

## Data & Repository

- **Figshare** -- research data publishing (dataset upload, file types supported, metadata: title, authors, description, categories, tags, license; DOI assignment, versioning, citation information, usage metrics: views, downloads, shares, altmetric score), data sharing (public/private/embargoed access, institutional repository integration)
- **Dryad** -- open data repository for research datasets (dataset submission: data files, metadata, README, associated publication DOI, funding information; DOI assignment, CC0 license, curation review, download metrics, data reuse tracking)
- **Zenodo (CERN)** -- general-purpose open research repository (record types: publication, dataset, software, poster, presentation, image, video, lesson; metadata: title, creators, description, access rights, license, DOI, related identifiers, communities, grants; versioning, usage statistics)
- **Dataverse** -- research data sharing (dataverse hierarchy: institution, department, project; dataset: title, authors, description, subject, keyword, geographic coverage, time period; file-level metadata, download statistics), citation (persistent identifiers: DOI, Handle; citation format, machine-actionable citation), preservation (file format preservation, metadata standards, data integrity checks)
- **Institutional repositories (DSpace, Digital Commons)** -- theses and dissertations (title, author, advisor, department, degree, date, abstract, keywords, full-text PDF, embargo status), faculty publications (article metadata, post-print/pre-print deposits, publisher policy compliance, Open Access status), institutional output (annual deposit counts, downloads, geographic reach, top items)

## Impact & Metrics

- **Altmetric** -- alternative research impact metrics (Altmetric Attention Score, source breakdown: news outlets, blogs, Twitter/X, Facebook, Wikipedia, policy documents, patents, Reddit, peer review platforms; demographic breakdown by geography, researcher vs. public), social media mentions (tweet count, Facebook posts, blog posts, news mentions), news coverage (media outlet, article title, date, reach estimate)
- **PlumX (Elsevier)** -- research impact metrics across categories: usage (abstract views, full-text views, downloads, clicks, holdings, ILL requests), captures (bookmarks, favorites, readers, watchers, forks), mentions (blog posts, news, comments, reviews, Wikipedia references), social media (tweets, Facebook likes, shares, Reddit posts), citations (citation count, Scopus, CrossRef, patent citations, clinical citations)
- **SciVal (Elsevier)** -- research performance benchmarking (publication count, citation count, field-weighted citation impact, outputs in top percentiles, collaboration rates), collaboration analytics (institutional, national, international collaboration percentages, co-authorship networks, top collaborating institutions), topic and subject area analysis (research strengths, topic clusters, emerging topics, competitive benchmarking)
- **InCites (Clarivate)** -- citation-based research analytics (document counts, times cited, category normalized citation impact, percentage of documents in top 1%/10%, h-index), institutional benchmarking (peer comparison, research area profiles, collaboration networks, funding acknowledgment analysis, open access percentage), research area profiles (publication trends by Web of Science category, citation impact by discipline)
