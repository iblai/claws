# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for financial aid administration.

## Financial Aid Management

- **Ellucian Banner Financial Aid** -- aid packaging (award codes, fund sources, amounts, packaging group, budget components), award letters (cost of attendance, EFC/SAI, need, awards by type, acceptance status), fund management (fund codes, budgets, allocations, available balance, disbursement caps), disbursement tracking (scheduled date, disbursement date, amount, hold reason, refund status), satisfactory academic progress (SAP status, GPA, pace/completion rate, maximum timeframe, appeal status), loan entrance/exit counseling completion, document tracking (verification documents, missing items, deadlines)
- **Ellucian Colleague Financial Aid** -- need analysis (cost of attendance components, EFC/SAI, unmet need, auto-zero EFC indicators), award processing (packaging rules, fund priorities, award sequences, batch packaging results), SAP evaluation (term GPA, cumulative GPA, pace calculation, quantitative/qualitative measures, probation/suspension status), student budget overrides, professional judgment adjustments, loan certification
- **Oracle PeopleSoft Financial Aid** -- packaging (auto-packaging rules, award tables, fund hierarchies, equity packaging), disbursement (disbursement plans, split codes, disbursement dates, holds, cancellations), regulatory reporting (FISAP, IPEDS, state reporting), budget and cost of attendance tables, aggregate loan tracking, return of Title IV calculations
- **Workday Student Financial Aid** -- aid lifecycle management (application through disbursement workflow status), compliance tracking (federal/state regulation adherence, audit trail, policy engine results), packaging optimization (rule-based packaging, award simulations, budget impact analysis), real-time fund utilization, integrated student billing
- **Jenzabar One Financial Aid** -- award management (fund setup, packaging rules, batch awarding, individual adjustments), fund tracking (allocations, commitments, disbursements, refunds, remaining balance), regulatory compliance (federal methodology, SAP rules, R2T4 calculations), communication management (missing document letters, award notifications)
- **PowerFAIDS** -- aid packaging (auto-packaging engine, awarding rules, fund priorities, packaging groups), need analysis (federal methodology, institutional methodology, custom formulas), award optimization (financial aid leveraging, discount rate modeling, net revenue analysis), batch processing (ISIR loading, packaging runs, SAP evaluations), reporting (federal, state, institutional, ad hoc)

## Federal & State Aid Systems

- **FAFSA (Federal Application for Student Aid)** -- Institutional Student Information Records (ISIRs): student demographics, dependency status, household size, income (AGI, taxes paid, untaxed income), asset data (cash, investments, business/farm net worth), number in college, Expected Family Contribution (EFC) / Student Aid Index (SAI), Pell Grant eligibility, verification selection flag and tracking group, reject codes, comment codes, assumption codes, match flags (SSA, DHS, VA, NSLDS, SSS), professional judgment indicators, multi-school ISIR routing
- **COD (Common Origination and Disbursement)** -- Pell Grant origination (award amount, enrollment status, COA, EFC, scheduled award, LEU percentage), Direct Loan origination (loan type: subsidized/unsubsidized/PLUS/Grad PLUS, loan amount, loan period, interest rate, origination fee, disbursement amounts and dates, MPNI status), disbursement records (actual disbursement amounts, dates, adjustments, returns), school reconciliation data (COD vs. institution records), TEACH Grant origination
- **NSLDS (National Student Loan Data System)** -- aggregate loan history (loan type, outstanding principal, total disbursed, loan status, servicer), Pell lifetime eligibility used (LEU percentage, remaining eligibility), overpayment flags, transfer monitoring/financial aid history, Subsidized Usage Limit Applicability (SULA), defaulted loan indicators, enrollment reporting discrepancies, TEACH Grant service obligation status
- **CSS Profile (College Board)** -- institutional methodology need analysis (parent income and assets with detail: home equity, business equity, retirement accounts, medical expenses), non-custodial parent data (income, assets, household, other children's education costs), student income and assets, special circumstance explanations, institutional questions, supplemental form data, estimated institutional contribution
- **State grant portals** -- state-specific grant eligibility determinations (residency verification, enrollment level, GPA requirements, program eligibility), award notifications (grant name, amount, conditions), compliance reporting (certification deadlines, enrollment verification, disbursement reporting), state-specific application data (e.g., Cal Grant GPA verification, Texas TASFA, New York TAP)

## Scholarship Management

- **AcademicWorks** -- scholarship matching (eligibility criteria, auto-match results, student opportunity lists), application management (application forms, essay submissions, supporting documents, recommender submissions), donor reporting (fund utilization, recipient demographics, impact metrics, stewardship reports), renewal tracking (renewal criteria, GPA requirements, enrollment verification, renewal decisions), awarding workflows (review committee assignments, scoring rubrics, award decisions, notification status)
- **Blackbaud Award Management** -- scholarship workflows (application review, selection committee scoring, award approval chain), fund management (endowed fund balances, spending policy, payout calculations, underwater fund alerts), donor stewardship (thank-you letter generation, scholarship recipient profiles, fund performance reports), reporting (awards by fund, by department, by demographic, unawarded funds)
- **ScholarshipUniverse** -- scholarship matching engine (rules-based matching, student profile auto-population, eligibility scoring), application aggregation (single student profile, multi-scholarship applications, document reuse), analytics (application rates, conversion rates, demographic coverage, fund utilization)
- **NextGen Web Solutions** -- scholarship applications (online forms, essay collection, transcript upload, recommendation requests), selection committee tools (rubric scoring, committee commenting, comparative ranking, voting), award notification and acceptance workflow

## Loan Management & Servicing

- **ECSI (Heartland)** -- institutional loan management (Perkins Loan records, institutional loan portfolios, promissory notes), billing (statements, payment due dates, amounts, payment history), servicing status (in-school deferment, grace period, repayment, delinquency, default), exit interview completion, assignment and liquidation tracking
- **Nelnet** -- loan servicing (current balance, interest rate, repayment plan type, payment amount, due date), repayment plans (standard, graduated, extended, income-driven), borrower communication (correspondence history, contact attempts, payment reminders), account status (current, delinquent, forbearance, deferment)
- **Great Lakes / Ascendium** -- federal loan servicing (account balance, payment history, repayment plan, servicer transfer records), default prevention (delinquency alerts, counseling touchpoints, rehabilitation agreements), borrower demographics and contact information

## Verification & Compliance

- **IRS Data Retrieval Tool (DRT)** -- tax return data for FAFSA verification (AGI, taxes paid, income earned from work, IRS request flag, DRT usage indicator), transfer status (successful, not transferred, amended return flag), IRS data match results
- **Inceptia / ECMC** -- verification processing (document collection status, conflicting information resolution, verification results), default prevention (cohort default rate analytics, early intervention contacts, financial literacy program completion), loan counseling records
- **CampusLogic (now Anthology Reach)** -- verification document collection (tax returns, W-2s, verification worksheets, identity statements, household size confirmation), workflow automation (task assignments, escalation rules, automated communications, status tracking), student self-service portal (document upload, electronic signatures, form completion, status checking), compliance dashboards (verification completion rates, processing times, bottleneck identification)
- **NASFAA resources** -- regulatory guidance references (Dear Colleague Letters, Federal Register notices, handbook updates), federal methodology calculation references, professional judgment documentation templates, audit preparation resources

## Reporting & Analytics

- **FISAP (Fiscal Operations Report)** -- annual federal reporting for campus-based programs (Federal Work-Study, FSEOG, Perkins: expenditures, recipients, compensation, fund balances, carry-forward amounts), institutional and student data sections, program participation indicators
- **IPEDS** -- institutional financial aid data reporting (percent receiving aid by type, average award amounts, net price by income quintile, grant and scholarship data, loan averages, cost of attendance components), Student Financial Aid survey component data
- **Tableau / Power BI** -- aid distribution dashboards (awards by type, source, demographic group, program, term), equity analysis (unmet need by race/ethnicity, income quintile, first-generation status, Pell-eligible vs. non-Pell), SAP tracking (pass rates, appeal rates, suspension rates by population), discount rate trending, yield analysis by aid package
