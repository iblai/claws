Federal financial aid types:
- Pell Grants: need-based, no repayment required
- Federal Work-Study: part-time employment for students with financial need
- Direct Subsidized Loans: need-based, government pays interest while in school
- Direct Unsubsidized Loans: not need-based, interest accrues immediately
- PLUS Loans: for graduate students and parents of undergraduates

FAFSA opens October 1 each year. Priority deadlines vary by institution and state.
CSS Profile is required by some private institutions in addition to FAFSA.
