# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for alumni relations and advancement.

## Alumni & Advancement CRM

- **Ellucian Advance** -- alumni records (constituent ID, name, class year, degree, major, contact information, deceased flag, do-not-contact flag, FERPA restrictions), giving history (gift date, amount, fund designation, gift type: cash, pledge, planned gift, matching; payment method, receipt number, campaign credit, appeal code), event participation (event name, date, attendance status, RSVP, guest count, meal preference), engagement scoring (engagement score, score components: giving, volunteering, events, communications; score tier, last engagement date), prospect management (prospect rating, capacity, inclination, readiness, assigned gift officer, moves management stage, solicitation history, ask amounts, proposal status)
- **Blackbaud Raiser's Edge NXT** -- donor management (constituent records: bio/demographics, addresses, phone numbers, email, relationships, education, employment; membership, attributes, notes), gift processing (gift entry: amount, date, fund, campaign, appeal, package; gift type: donation, pledge, recurring, planned, tribute, matching gift; receipt generation, acknowledgment letters, soft credits), prospect research (wealth screening results, capacity rating, inclination indicators, RFM scoring, giving history analysis), campaign tracking (campaign name, goal, raised-to-date, donor count, pledge pipeline, projections, solicitor assignments), tribute giving (tribute type: in honor/in memory, tribute name, notification recipient, acknowledgment)
- **Salesforce (with HEDA/EDA)** -- alumni engagement tracking (contact records, account affiliations, activity history, campaign membership, email engagement), relationship management (household accounts, organizational affiliations, relationship records, employment history, volunteer activities), event coordination (campaign events, registration, attendance, post-event surveys, engagement attribution), communication history (email sends/opens/clicks, mass email campaigns, call logs, meeting notes, task records)
- **Slate (Technolutions)** -- alumni communications (email campaigns, segmented distribution lists, template management, delivery/open/click tracking), event management (event creation, registration forms, capacity management, waitlists, post-event surveys), engagement tracking (portal logins, email engagement, event attendance, form submissions, volunteer activities; shared data model with admissions for full lifecycle view)
- **Anthology Encompass** -- alumni CRM (constituent profiles, communication preferences, engagement history, affinity groups), career services integration (alumni career data, mentoring availability, employer connections, job postings from alumni employers), mentoring programs (mentor/mentee matching, program enrollment, session tracking, relationship status, outcomes)

## Fundraising & Giving

- **Blackbaud CRM** -- major gift tracking (prospect assignments, relationship manager, strategy, ask date, ask amount, expected close date, gift stage, cultivation activities, proposal status, stewardship plan), planned giving (bequest intentions, trust types, life insurance policies, retirement plan beneficiary designations, maturity value estimates, documentation status), pledge management (pledge amount, payment schedule, installments received, outstanding balance, pledge reminders, write-off status), campaign progress (campaign hierarchy: overall, sub-campaigns, funds; goal vs. raised, donor counts, average gift, participation rates by constituency)
- **GiveCampus** -- online giving pages (giving form configuration, suggested amounts, recurring giving options, designation choices, matching gift prompts, tribute giving), crowdfunding (project pages, fundraising goal, amount raised, donor count, social sharing metrics, updates, stretch goals), giving day platforms (giving day event configuration, leaderboards, challenges, matching fund triggers, real-time donor counts, social media integration), text-to-give (keyword setup, mobile giving records, confirmation messages)
- **EverTrue** -- alumni engagement analytics (engagement score, social media activity, email engagement, event attendance, volunteering, communication preferences), social media enrichment (LinkedIn data: employer, title, location, education; Facebook engagement; Twitter mentions), giving propensity modeling (propensity score, capacity estimate, affinity indicators, likelihood to give, recommended ask amount, optimal solicitation timing)
- **Gravyty (now Anthology Gravyty)** -- AI-powered fundraising (AI-generated outreach suggestions, recommended talking points, optimal contact timing), donor engagement suggestions (prioritized outreach lists, engagement propensity scores, recommended channel: email, phone, visit), outreach prioritization (ranked prospect lists, engagement signals, giving window predictions, pipeline value estimates)
- **iWave / DonorSearch** -- prospect research (biographical data, business affiliations, board memberships, nonprofit involvement), wealth screening (estimated net worth, real estate holdings, stock ownership, compensation data, asset indicators), philanthropic indicators (giving history to other nonprofits, foundation board membership, political giving, annual fund participation rates), capacity ratings (estimated giving capacity, capacity range, confidence level, data sources used)

## Communication & Engagement

- **Hivebrite (formerly Graduway)** -- alumni community platform (member profiles, directory search, privacy settings, profile completeness), networking (connection requests, messaging, introduction requests, alumni search by location/industry/class year), mentoring (mentor/mentee profiles, matching criteria, program enrollment, meeting scheduling, session logs, outcome tracking), job board (alumni-posted positions, job applications, employer profiles, industry categories), groups (affinity groups, chapter groups, interest-based groups, group membership, discussion boards, event calendars)
- **PeopleGrove** -- mentoring and networking platform (mentor profiles: expertise, career history, availability, mentoring style; mentee profiles: goals, interests, class year), career connections (career path exploration, informational interview requests, job shadowing, referrals), community engagement (discussion forums, resource sharing, event promotion, newsletter integration)
- **Mailchimp / Constant Contact / Emma** -- email campaigns (campaign name, subject line, send date, recipient list, send count, open rate, click rate, bounce rate, unsubscribe rate), newsletter distribution (newsletter template, content sections, featured stories, event promotions, giving links), engagement analytics (subscriber engagement scoring, click maps, device/client breakdowns, geographic data, A/B test results, list growth trends, deliverability metrics)
- **Anthology Encompass** -- alumni communications (targeted outreach by affinity, class year, giving history, location, interests), targeted outreach (segmentation criteria, campaign targeting, communication preferences, channel optimization), engagement scoring (multi-channel engagement tracking, score calculation methodology, tier classification, engagement trend over time)

## Events Management

- **Eventbrite** -- event registration (event name, date, location, ticket types, pricing, capacity, registration count, waitlist), ticketing (ticket tiers: general admission, VIP, early bird; pricing, discount codes, group rates, refund policy), attendee management (attendee list, check-in status, dietary preferences, accessibility needs, contact information, transfer/refund requests)
- **Cvent** -- event management (event creation, agenda, sessions, speakers, sponsors, venue details, budget tracking), registration (registration types, custom questions, payment processing, confirmation emails, badge generation), venue sourcing (venue search, RFP management, contract negotiation, room blocks), attendee engagement (event app, session check-in, polling, Q&A, networking, post-event surveys, CE/CPE credits)
- **Blackbaud NetCommunity** -- online event registration (event pages, registration forms, fee collection, capacity management, waitlists), donation forms (online giving pages, designation options, recurring giving setup, tribute giving, employer matching), alumni web portals (class pages, alumni directory, news and updates, giving history, event history)
- **Almabase** -- alumni events (event creation, registration, promotion, attendance tracking, photo galleries), email campaigns (template design, audience segmentation, send scheduling, performance analytics), giving days (giving day page, countdown timer, leaderboards, challenge matching, social sharing, real-time progress), class pages (class landing pages, reunion information, class notes, in memoriam, class giving)

## Data Enrichment & Research

- **LinkedIn Alumni Tool** -- alumni career tracking (current employer, job title, industry, location, years in role, career progression), geographic distribution (alumni locations by city, state, country; concentration maps), industry trends (industry distribution, trending industries, emerging employers, career path patterns from graduation to current role)
- **EverTrue** -- social media data enrichment (LinkedIn profile data, Facebook engagement signals, Twitter activity, profile photo updates, employment changes), engagement scoring (multi-channel score: giving, events, email, social, volunteering; score trend, peer comparison), contact information updates (address verification, email validation, phone number discovery, employer updates, deceased identification)
- **iWave** -- wealth indicators (estimated net worth, income estimates, stock holdings, SEC insider filings), giving capacity (estimated annual giving capacity, lifetime capacity, capacity range, confidence rating), philanthropic history (gifts to other nonprofits, foundation affiliations, donor-advised fund activity, political contributions), real estate data (property ownership, property values, purchase dates, mortgage information, geographic location)
- **National Student Clearinghouse** -- degree verification (degree type, major, conferral date, honors, institution name), enrollment history (institutions attended, dates, enrollment level, full/part-time), continuing education tracking (post-baccalaureate enrollment, graduate program attendance, additional degrees earned, professional certifications)

## Reporting & Analytics

- **CASE (Council for Advancement and Support of Education)** -- VSE Survey data (total giving by source: alumni, non-alumni individuals, corporations, foundations, other organizations; by purpose: current operations, capital, endowment; gift counts, donor counts), fundraising benchmarks (median and mean giving by institution type/size/control, alumni participation rates, cost to raise a dollar), alumni engagement metrics (CASE Alumni Engagement Metrics: experiential engagement, philanthropic engagement, volunteer engagement, communication engagement)
- **Tableau / Power BI** -- giving dashboards (total giving by fiscal year, donor counts, average gift size, gift distribution by fund/campaign/source, monthly giving trends), alumni engagement scoring (engagement score distribution, score by class year/geographic region/affinity group, score trend over time, engagement tier migration), campaign performance (campaign goal tracking, pace to goal, solicitor activity, proposal pipeline, donor retention rate), reunion participation (reunion attendance rates by class year, reunion giving participation, class agent activity, pre-reunion vs. reunion-year giving)
