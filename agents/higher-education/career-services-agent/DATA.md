# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for career services and professional development.

## Career Management Platforms

- **Handshake** -- job and internship postings (job title, employer, location, job type: full-time/part-time/internship/co-op, salary range, application deadline, required qualifications, job description), employer connections (employer name, industry, recruiter contacts, relationship status, recruiting agreements, on-campus presence), career fair management (fair name, date, employer registrations, student registrations, check-in data, booth assignments, industry representation), on-campus recruiting (interview schedules, room reservations, student sign-ups, interview outcomes, employer feedback), application tracking (jobs applied, application date, application status, employer response, interview invitations), student profiles (major, GPA, class year, work authorization, skills, work experience, career interests, resume uploads, profile completeness score)
- **Symplicity CSM** -- job board (posted positions, employer, job type, location, application method, posting expiration, student applications), employer relations (employer contact records, interaction history, partnership agreements, site visit logs, sponsorship data), counseling appointments (appointment date, counselor, student, session type: resume review, mock interview, career exploration, job search strategy; notes, follow-up tasks), event management (career fairs, workshops, info sessions, employer panels; registration, attendance, satisfaction surveys), outcomes tracking (first destination data, employment status, employer, job title, salary, location, continuing education plans, start date)
- **12Twenty** -- employment outcomes (graduation term, employment status: employed, seeking, continuing education, not seeking; employer name, job title, salary, industry, location, full-time/part-time, how found), salary data (reported salary, salary ranges by major/industry/region, median salary by program, year-over-year trends), career coaching (coaching session records, session type, action items, student goals, progress tracking), employer engagement (employer interactions, recruiting activity, hiring history, relationship health score)
- **GradLeaders** -- MBA/graduate career management (student profiles, career goals, industry preferences, target companies), employer pipelines (employer relationship stages, recruiting timeline, offer tracking, acceptance rates), outcomes reporting (placement rates, salary data, time-to-placement, industry distribution, geographic distribution), internship and fellowship tracking

## Job Boards & Labor Market Data

- **LinkedIn** -- job postings (title, company, location, description, required skills, experience level, salary range, application URL), alumni network (alumni profiles by employer, industry, location, role; career trajectories, connections), industry trends (hiring trends by industry/role, skills in demand, company growth indicators), skills data (skill endorsements, skill frequency by job posting, emerging skills, skills gap analysis)
- **Indeed** -- job market trends (posting volumes by occupation, location, industry; trending job titles), salary comparisons (salary estimates by title, location, experience level, company), posting volumes by field (demand indicators, seasonal hiring patterns, remote work prevalence)
- **Glassdoor** -- company reviews (overall rating, culture rating, work-life balance, compensation/benefits, career opportunities, management quality), interview questions (reported interview questions by company and role, difficulty rating, interview process description, offer rates), salary data (reported salaries by title, company, location, experience level, base pay, bonus, equity)
- **Burning Glass / Lightcast (EMSI)** -- labor market analytics (job posting analytics, employer demand by region/occupation, real-time labor market data), in-demand skills (hard skills, soft skills, certifications, tools/technologies ranked by frequency in postings), occupation projections (10-year employment growth, annual openings, automation risk, education requirements), regional employment data (employment by industry/occupation in MSA/state/national, wage data, cost of living, employer concentrations)
- **O*NET (Department of Labor)** -- occupation descriptions (summary, tasks, technology skills, tools used, work activities, work context), skill requirements (knowledge areas, skills, abilities, work styles, work values rated by importance and level), career pathways (related occupations, career changers matrix, bright outlook occupations, green economy occupations), wage data (median, 10th/25th/75th/90th percentile by occupation and geography)
- **BLS Occupational Outlook Handbook** -- employment projections (projected growth rate, projected openings, employment change), median pay (annual salary, hourly wage by occupation), education requirements (typical entry-level education, on-the-job training, work experience requirements), similar occupations, state and area data

## Resume & Professional Development

- **VMock** -- AI-powered resume review (overall SMART score 0-100, section scores: impact, presentation, competencies), targeted improvement suggestions (bullet-level feedback, action verb recommendations, quantification prompts, formatting corrections, keyword optimization for target industries), benchmark comparisons (score relative to peer group, industry-specific scoring)
- **Big Interview** -- mock interview practice (recorded video responses, AI-generated feedback, response timing, filler word detection), question library (behavioral questions, technical questions, industry-specific questions, competency-based questions by role/industry), AI feedback (response structure analysis, STAR method adherence, confidence indicators, content relevance scoring)
- **Quinncia** -- resume analysis (formatting score, content score, ATS compatibility, keyword density, section completeness), interview simulation (AI interview sessions, response evaluation, body language analysis, speech pattern feedback), career readiness assessment (skills inventory, career exploration recommendations, development plan)
- **Portfolium (now Instructure)** -- student ePortfolios (project showcases, work samples, reflections, multimedia uploads), project showcases (project title, description, media, tags, course association, skills demonstrated), competency badges (badge name, issuing criteria, skills validated, evidence artifacts, badge display settings)

## Experiential Learning & Internships

- **Handshake / Symplicity** -- internship postings (position title, employer, duration, paid/unpaid, hourly rate, learning objectives, supervisor), co-op positions (alternating/parallel schedule, employer, compensation, academic credit, evaluation), experiential learning tracking (experience type: internship, co-op, service learning, practicum, clinical; dates, supervisor, hours, site, learning outcomes)
- **Parker Dewey** -- micro-internships (project title, employer, project description, required skills, compensation, timeline, deliverables, application status), short-term project opportunities (duration, estimated hours, remote/on-site, industry, skill development areas)
- **Riipen** -- experiential learning projects (project title, industry partner, project scope, deliverables, timeline, team size), industry partner matching (partner company, project needs, student skill alignment, faculty sponsor), project outcomes (deliverables completed, partner satisfaction, student reflections, skills gained)
- **NACE (National Association of Colleges and Employers)** -- First Destination Survey data (response rates, employment rates, salary medians by major/college, continuing education rates, knowledge rates), salary benchmarks (starting salary by degree level, major, industry, region, institution type), career readiness competencies (competency framework: critical thinking, communication, teamwork, technology, leadership, professionalism, equity, career self-development; assessment rubrics)

## Alumni & Employer Relations

- **Graduway (now Hivebrite)** -- alumni mentoring networks (mentor profiles, mentee matching criteria, mentorship status, session logs, communication history), community engagement (alumni directory participation, group memberships, content contributions), career networking (job referrals, introduction requests, networking event participation)
- **PeopleGrove** -- mentorship matching (mentor/mentee profiles, matching algorithm criteria, relationship status, meeting logs, goal tracking), alumni career connections (career path data, industry expertise, willingness to advise, availability), community building (community groups, discussion threads, resource sharing, event participation)
- **Salesforce / HubSpot** -- employer relationship management (employer account records, contact information, interaction history, partnership tier, recruiting agreement terms), recruiting event tracking (events attended, sponsored, students engaged, follow-up activities, conversion to hires)

## Outcomes & Reporting

- **NACE First Destination Survey** -- post-graduation employment data (employment status at graduation and 6 months out, employer name, job title, industry NAICS code, location, full-time/part-time), salary outcomes (starting salary, bonus, signing bonus by major/college/degree level), continuing education rates (graduate school enrollment, institution, program, degree type), knowledge rate and response rate by program, outcomes by demographic group
- **Tableau / Power BI** -- career outcomes dashboards (placement rates by major/college/degree, salary distributions, industry and employer breakdowns, geographic dispersion maps), employer engagement analytics (employer activity levels, recruiting event attendance, hires per employer, relationship health trends), appointment utilization (appointments by type, counselor, student demographics, no-show rates, satisfaction scores, peak demand periods)
