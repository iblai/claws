# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for student services and campus life.

## Housing & Residence Life

- **StarRez** -- room assignments (building, floor, room number, room type: single/double/suite/apartment, bed space, occupancy status, assignment dates), housing applications (application date, term, preferences: building, room type, roommate requests, lifestyle questionnaire responses, special accommodations), roommate matching (compatibility scores, preference alignment, mutual requests, conflict history), bed space inventory (total capacity, available spaces by building/room type/gender, occupancy rates, vacancy tracking), meal plan assignments (plan type, swipe count, dining dollar balance, plan changes, effective dates), housing contracts (contract period, cancellation requests, early termination, break housing approvals), room condition reports (check-in/check-out inspections, damage assessments, billing charges)
- **Symplicity Housing** -- housing lottery (lottery number, priority tier, selection round, lottery date), room selection (available rooms, selection timestamp, room holds, swap requests), contract management (contract terms, signing status, cancellation policy, financial obligations, refund eligibility)
- **RMS (Residential Management System)** -- maintenance requests (request type: plumbing, electrical, HVAC, furniture, pest; location, submitted date, priority, assigned technician, status, completion date), room condition reports (pre/post inspection items, damage descriptions, photos, charge assessments), key management (key issuance, returns, lost key reports, re-core requests, lockout assistance)
- **eRezLife** -- housing applications (application form responses, term, preferences, accessibility needs), room assignments (building, room, assignment date, change history), roommate preferences (lifestyle preferences, sleep schedule, study habits, guest policy, cleanliness), RA duty schedules and incident reports

## Dining Services

- **CBORD** -- meal plan management (plan type, meals remaining, reset schedule, plan changes, rollover policy), dining dollar balances (current balance, transaction history, declining balance rate, auto-replenish settings), nutritional information (calorie counts, macronutrients, allergens: gluten, dairy, nuts, soy, shellfish; dietary labels: vegan, vegetarian, halal, kosher), menu data (daily menus by location, station, meal period; rotating menu cycles, special event menus)
- **Transact (formerly Blackboard Transact)** -- campus card transactions (transaction type, amount, location, date/time, running balance), meal swipes (swipe location, time, meal period, guest swipes used, daily/weekly swipe limits), dining locations (location name, hours, stations, meal periods, capacity), declining balance (balance, deposits, spending history, average daily spend)
- **NetNutrition** -- menu publishing (daily/weekly menus by dining location and station), allergen data (allergen flags per menu item, ingredient lists, cross-contamination warnings), nutritional breakdowns by dining location (calories, fat, carbs, protein, sodium, fiber per serving, serving size)

## Campus Card & Access

- **Transact / CBORD** -- campus ID card management (card issuance, replacement, deactivation, photo, card status), building access (access permissions by building/zone, access time restrictions, access logs with timestamps), vending transactions (machine ID, item, amount, date/time), laundry (machine availability, cycle start/end, payment amount), printing balances (print credits, usage by printer location, color vs. B&W, page counts)
- **Allegion (Schlage / ENGAGE)** -- door access schedules (access time windows, day-of-week rules, holiday exceptions, emergency lockdown overrides), building entry logs (card swipe records: card ID, door, timestamp, access granted/denied, reason codes)
- **Lenel / LenelS2** -- access control (cardholder records, access level assignments, access group memberships, temporary access grants), security badge management (badge issuance, expiration, activation/deactivation, visitor badges, contractor badges), event monitoring (door forced open, door held open, access denied, alarm events)

## Health & Wellness

- **Medicat** -- health center appointments (appointment type, provider, date/time, status, check-in, chief complaint category), immunization compliance (required immunizations, submission status, waiver requests, hold placement for non-compliance, deadline tracking), insurance verification (insurance carrier, policy number, coverage status, waiver eligibility, enrollment/waiver deadline), student health forms (health history questionnaire, allergy information, medication list, emergency contacts)
- **Titanium Schedule** -- counseling center scheduling (appointment date/time, counselor, session type: intake, individual, group, crisis; modality: in-person, telehealth), group sessions (group topic, schedule, capacity, enrollment, attendance), crisis management (walk-in availability, crisis triage, safety assessment, referral to hospital/community resources, follow-up scheduling)
- **Point and Click (PnC)** -- student health records (encounter notes, diagnosis codes, treatment plans, referrals; HIPAA-protected), pharmacy (prescriptions, refills, dispensing records, drug interactions), lab orders (lab tests ordered, specimen collection, results, follow-up instructions)
- **JED Foundation / Kognito** -- mental health training resources (training modules, completion tracking, certificate issuance), QPR gatekeeper modules (training enrollment, completion status, refresher schedules, campus trainer assignments)

## Parking & Transportation

- **T2 Systems** -- parking permits (permit type: student, faculty, staff, visitor, ADA; zone, lot, effective dates, vehicle information, permit cost, payment status), citation management (citation number, violation type, date, time, location, vehicle, fine amount, payment status), appeals (appeal submission, reason, supporting documents, hearing date, decision, status), lot availability (real-time space counts by lot, occupancy percentage, time-of-day patterns)
- **ParkMobile** -- mobile parking payment (zone number, session start/end times, duration, rate, payment amount, receipt), session management (active sessions, session extensions, notification preferences, payment method)
- **TransLoc / Rider** -- shuttle tracking (real-time vehicle locations, route assignments, estimated arrival times, stop sequences), route schedules (route name, stops, departure times, frequency, service days, special event routes), rider analytics (ridership counts by route/stop/time, peak usage periods, route demand data)
- **Lyft / Uber campus programs** -- ride subsidies (eligible ride zones, subsidy amount per ride, ride limits per period, program budget utilization), safe ride programs (eligible hours, pickup/dropoff zones, ride request history, usage patterns)

## Student Activities & Organizations

- **Presence (formerly OrgSync / CollegiateLink)** -- student organization management (org name, category, status: active/inactive, officers, advisor, constitution, annual registration), event registration (event name, date, location, capacity, attendees, check-in method), involvement tracking (organizations joined, events attended, hours logged, positions held, involvement hours by term), co-curricular transcripts (verified activities, leadership positions, service hours, skills and competencies, award/honor records)
- **CampusGroups** -- club management (club profile, membership roster, officer roles, meeting schedules, budget allocation), event calendars (events by organization, date, location, category, attendance tracking), membership rosters (member name, join date, role, activity level, dues status)
- **Symplicity CSM** -- student engagement tracking (involvement data, leadership positions, community service hours), service tracking (service project records, partner organizations, hours logged, impact metrics)

## Facilities & Campus Operations

- **SchoolDude (Brightly)** -- maintenance work orders (request type, location, description, priority, submitted by, assigned technician, status, completion date, labor hours, parts used), facility reservations (space name, date, time, event type, requestor, setup requirements, AV needs, catering), event setup requests (room configuration, furniture needs, signage, audio/visual equipment, post-event breakdown)
- **EMS (Event Management System)** -- room reservations (building, room, date, time, duration, event name, host organization, expected attendance, recurring flag), event scheduling (event type, setup/teardown times, catering orders, AV requirements, special accommodations), space availability (real-time room availability, capacity, room features, equipment inventory, blackout dates)
- **AiM (AssetWorks)** -- facilities management (building profiles, system inventories, preventive maintenance schedules, equipment lifecycle data), space utilization (room usage rates, peak usage times, underutilized spaces, classroom utilization metrics), work order tracking (request submission, prioritization, assignment, parts ordering, completion, cost tracking)

## Recreation & Athletics

- **Fusion / InnoSoft** -- recreation center access (entry swipes, visit frequency, peak usage times, member type), intramural registration (sport, team name, roster, captain, division, season, game schedule), equipment checkout (item name, checkout date, due date, return date, condition, borrower ID), fitness class scheduling (class name, instructor, date, time, capacity, waitlist, drop-in availability, attendance)
- **IMLeagues** -- intramural sports leagues (sport, season, division, league rules, registration deadlines), team registration (team name, captain, roster, division placement, eligibility verification), game schedules (matchups, date, time, location, officials, scores, standings, playoff brackets)
