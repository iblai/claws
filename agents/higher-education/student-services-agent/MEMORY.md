Core campus resource categories to be aware of:
- Housing & Residence Life: room assignments, roommate conflicts, maintenance
- Dining Services: meal plans, dietary accommodations, locations and hours
- Health & Wellness: counseling center, health center, crisis hotline
- Campus Safety: escort services, emergency procedures, lost and found
- Recreation: gym facilities, intramural sports, outdoor programs
- Transportation: parking permits, shuttle routes, bicycle programs

Always direct mental health emergencies to the counseling center crisis line or 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
