# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for academic advising.

## Student Information Systems (SIS)

- **Ellucian Banner** -- student records (student ID, name, demographics, contact info), course history (term, CRN, subject, course number, title, grade, credits earned, repeat indicator), cumulative and term GPA, enrollment status (full-time, part-time, half-time, withdrawn), academic standing (good standing, probation, suspension, dean's list), holds (advising, financial, disciplinary, with hold type, amount, originator, reason), degree program (college, major, minor, concentration, catalog year, expected graduation term), advisor assignment, FERPA restrictions, transfer credit evaluations
- **Ellucian Colleague** -- academic records (course history, grades, quality points, GPA by term and cumulative), registration data (current term schedule, credits registered, waitlisted courses), transcript data (official transcript content, transfer credits, institutional credits, degree awarded), academic plan (program, major, minor, specialization, catalog year), attendance flags, student communication logs
- **Oracle PeopleSoft Campus Solutions** -- student academics (academic career, program, plan, sub-plan, academic level, expected graduation term), enrollment data (classes enrolled, units taken, grading basis, enrollment status), degree progress (milestones completed, requirements satisfied, candidacy status), student group assignments (honors, athletes, veterans, first-generation), advisor committee assignments, service indicators (holds and positive indicators)
- **Workday Student** -- academic records (program of study, course registrations, grades, credits, GPA), course enrollment (section enrollment, waitlist position, swap eligibility), degree tracking (requirements completed, in-progress, remaining), academic calendar integration, student profile (demographics, contact, emergency contacts, advisor)
- **Jenzabar One** -- student academic profiles (major, minor, advisor, catalog year, class level), registration data (current and historical enrollments, schedule, room assignments), grading (midterm grades, final grades, incomplete contracts, grade changes), academic standing and honors designations, communication and notes history

## Degree Audit & Planning

- **Ellucian DegreeWorks** -- degree audit reports (requirements block: institution, college, major, minor, concentration, general education; status: complete, in-progress, not yet taken; courses applied to each requirement), what-if analysis (simulated audits for major/minor changes, transfer credit scenarios), Student Educational Planner (SEP: term-by-term course plan, planned courses, planned terms, milestone markers, critical path courses), GPA calculators (graduation GPA, term GPA, repeat/replace scenarios), exception processing (course substitutions, waivers, forced course applications), Scribe block structure and rule definitions
- **CollegeSource TES & uAchieve** -- transfer equivalency (sending institution, sending course, receiving course, credit hours, grade conversion, effective dates, expiration), degree audit (requirement trees, course applications, exceptions, in-progress tracking), academic planning tools (multi-term planning, prerequisite chain visualization), curriculum maps
- **Stellic** -- degree planning (interactive drag-and-drop course planner, prerequisite enforcement, co-requisite validation), course sequencing (optimal course order, bottleneck identification, time-to-degree estimation), graduation pathway optimization (credit efficiency analysis, summer/winter session recommendations, minor/double-major feasibility), what-if scenarios, real-time requirement checking
- **Anthology Degree Compass** -- course recommendation engine (personalized course suggestions based on academic history, major requirements, and predictive success scores), predictive analytics for course success (per-student grade predictions by course, confidence intervals), momentum indicators, optimal enrollment recommendations

## Advising & Student Success Platforms

- **EAB Navigate** -- advising appointments (appointment type, date/time, advisor, student, location/modality, check-in status, no-show tracking), early alerts (alert type: academic, attendance, financial, personal; raised by, date, severity, course context, intervention notes), student success campaigns (campaign name, target population, messaging, student responses, appointment conversions), notes and reports (advising session notes, student action items, follow-up flags, summary reports), progress reports (faculty-submitted feedback on attendance, participation, grade concerns), student profile summary (risk score, success markers, engagement indicators)
- **Civitas Learning (now Anthology)** -- predictive analytics (persistence likelihood score, term-to-term and year-to-year predictions, graduation probability), momentum metrics (credit accumulation pace, gateway course completion, GPA trajectory), student risk scores (low/medium/high risk classification, contributing factors, trend direction), intervention impact analysis (program effectiveness, ROI, before/after comparisons)
- **Starfish (now Hobsons)** -- early alert flags (flag type: attendance, grade, participation, referral; raiser, course section, date, notes), kudos (positive reinforcement flags from faculty), referrals (to tutoring, counseling, financial aid, career services; referral type, urgency, notes), appointment scheduling (advisor availability, student booking, appointment type, location), progress surveys (faculty survey responses on student performance, mid-term grade reports)
- **SSC Campus (now EAB)** -- advising workflows (caseload assignments, outreach queues, follow-up tasks), intervention tracking (intervention type, date, outcome, follow-up needed), caseload management (assigned students, risk tiers, engagement status, last contact date), student success indicators (course completion, credit accumulation, registration status)
- **Salesforce Advisor Link** -- advising case management (case records, interaction logs, action items, case status, resolution), student engagement tracking (touchpoint history, communication channel, sentiment, follow-up schedule), appointment management, reporting dashboards (caseload metrics, student outcomes, advisor activity)

## Course Catalog & Registration

- **Ellucian Banner Registration** -- course sections (CRN, subject, course number, section, title, credits, schedule type, instructional method), seat availability (enrollment count, maximum enrollment, waitlist count, waitlist capacity), waitlists (waitlist position, notification status, auto-enrollment settings), registration holds (hold type, hold reason, from/to dates, override capability), registration time tickets (registration start date/time by student group), prerequisite and co-requisite checking, repeat rules, cross-listed sections
- **CourseLeaf (Leepfrog Technologies)** -- course catalog management (course descriptions, prerequisites, co-requisites, credit hours, grading mode, offered terms, department), curriculum workflow (new course proposals, course modifications, program changes, approval chain status, effective catalog year), program requirement changes
- **Ad Astra / CollegeScheduler** -- course scheduling optimization (room assignments, instructor assignments, time blocks, section capacity), student schedule building (preferred times, course combinations, gap minimization, campus proximity), section demand forecasting, room utilization data
- **College Scheduler** -- student-facing schedule planner (drag-and-drop schedule creation, section comparison by time/instructor/location, break preferences, commute optimization), plan saving and sharing, waitlist alternatives

## Learning Management Systems (LMS)

- **Canvas (Instructure)** -- course grades (assignment scores, assignment group weights, current grade, final grade, grade history), assignment submissions (submission date, late status, submission type, attempt count, turnitin score), attendance (Roll Call attendance records, present/absent/late by date), engagement data (page views, participation metrics, time in course, last activity date, module progression)
- **Blackboard (Anthology)** -- gradebook data (grade columns, weighted totals, running averages, calculated grades), course activity (content access logs, time spent per item, login frequency), content access logs (which resources accessed, access count, last access date), early warning system indicators (rule-based alerts for low grades, missed assignments, inactivity)
- **Brightspace (D2L)** -- learner analytics (content access patterns, engagement heatmaps, at-risk indicators via Brightspace Insights), grade data (grade items, categories, weighted grades, grade schemes), content completion (module completion rates, content topic progress, checklist completion)
- **Moodle** -- course participation (activity completion tracking, event logs, login history), quiz scores (attempts, grades, time spent, question-level analysis), forum activity (posts, replies, discussion engagement metrics), resource access logs

## Analytics & Reporting

- **Tableau / Power BI** -- retention dashboards (first-to-second year retention, term-to-term persistence, by demographics and program), DFW rate analysis (courses with high D/F/Withdraw rates, DFW trends over time, DFW by instructor section, by student demographics), graduation pathway visualization (time-to-degree, credit-to-degree, pathway bottlenecks, excess credit analysis), advisor caseload and activity metrics
- **IPEDS** -- institutional comparison data (peer institution retention rates, graduation rates by demographic group, student-to-advisor ratios), graduation rates (4-year, 6-year, 8-year by cohort), retention benchmarks (full-time, part-time, by Pell status)
