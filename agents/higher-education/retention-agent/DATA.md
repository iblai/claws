# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for student retention and success.

## Student Success & Early Alert Platforms

- **EAB Navigate** -- early alerts (alert type: academic, attendance, financial, personal; raised by, course section, date, severity, narrative, recommended intervention, resolution status), risk scores (persistence likelihood, graduation probability, risk tier: low/medium/high, contributing risk factors, score trend over time), student success campaigns (campaign name, target population criteria, messaging content, delivery channel, student response rates, appointment conversion rates), advising notes (note date, author, category, content, follow-up flags, action items), appointment history (date, type, advisor, modality, check-in status, no-show flag, notes), case management (case type, assigned staff, status, interventions applied, outcome)
- **Civitas Learning (now Anthology)** -- persistence predictions (term-to-term persistence probability, year-to-year retention probability, graduation probability, model confidence level), momentum metrics (credit accumulation rate, credits earned vs. attempted, gateway course completion, GPA trajectory slope, course intensity ratio), student risk modeling (predictive risk score, primary risk drivers: academic, financial, engagement, personal; risk factor weights, cohort comparison), intervention impact analysis (intervention type, dosage, timing, pre/post outcome comparison, ROI estimate, statistical significance)
- **Starfish (now Hobsons)** -- early alert flags (flag type: low grade, missed class, low participation, personal concern, missing assignment; raiser: faculty/staff/system, course section, date, notes, severity), kudos (positive performance flags, faculty recognition, course context), referrals (to services: tutoring, counseling, financial aid, academic advising, career services; referral reason, urgency, follow-up status), progress surveys (faculty-completed surveys: attendance rating, assignment completion, class participation, current grade estimate, concern narrative), student tracking items (custom tracking flags, tag categories, cohort markers)
- **SSC Campus (now EAB)** -- caseload management (assigned students, caseload size, risk tier distribution, engagement status per student), intervention tracking (intervention type, date, staff member, student response, outcome code, follow-up needed), success markers (milestone achievements: orientation attended, advisor met, midterm grades above threshold, registration for next term, financial aid complete)

## Student Information Systems (SIS)

- **Ellucian Banner** -- enrollment status (full-time/part-time, enrolled/withdrawn/leave of absence, term-by-term enrollment history), course withdrawals (withdrawal date, course, reason code, last date of attendance, impact on financial aid), GPA trends (term GPA, cumulative GPA, institutional GPA, GPA by major courses, GPA trend direction over terms), holds (hold type: academic, financial, advising, disciplinary; hold reason, placed date, placed by, release date), academic standing changes (good standing, warning, probation, suspension, dismissal, effective term, appeal status), credit hour accumulation and pace
- **Ellucian Colleague** -- registration activity (courses added/dropped, dates, reasons, last date of attendance, census date status), grade history (term-by-term grades, grade changes, incomplete contracts, repeat/replace grades), attendance flags (system-generated or faculty-reported attendance concern indicators, last attendance date by course)
- **Oracle PeopleSoft Campus Solutions** -- enrollment patterns (full-time/part-time shifts, stop-out terms, summer enrollment, cross-registration), academic milestones (first-year seminar completion, gateway course completion, credit thresholds reached, major declaration, degree application), demographic data (age, gender, race/ethnicity, first-generation status, Pell eligibility, residency, distance from campus, high school type)
- **Workday Student** -- student lifecycle events (matriculation, enrollment changes, leave of absence, withdrawal, readmission, graduation), enrollment changes (section adds/drops, credit load adjustments, term withdrawal, date/time stamps), academic progress (GPA, credits earned, requirements met, academic plan status, advisor interactions)

## Learning Management Systems (LMS)

- **Canvas (Instructure)** -- login frequency (daily/weekly login counts, days since last login, login time patterns), assignment submission patterns (on-time submissions, late submissions, missing assignments, submission gaps, submission time relative to deadline), grade trends (assignment scores over time, running course grade, grade trajectory, below-threshold alerts), course engagement metrics (page views, participation score, time in course, discussion posts, content interactions, module progression rate)
- **Blackboard (Anthology)** -- content access logs (items accessed, access frequency, time spent per content item, last access date), discussion participation (posts created, replies, word count, peer interactions, discussion grade), assessment completion rates (quizzes started vs. completed, test attempts used, average scores, time per assessment), adaptive release tracking
- **Brightspace (D2L)** -- engagement analytics (Brightspace Insights dashboards: login trends, content access heat maps, submission patterns), at-risk indicators (Intelligent Agent triggers: inactivity thresholds, grade thresholds, submission deadlines missed), content interaction data (video views, page visits, tool usage, time-on-task by content type)
- **Moodle** -- activity logs (event-level logs: login, resource view, assignment submission, quiz attempt, forum post, with timestamps), forum participation (posts, replies, discussion subscriptions, peer ratings), resource access patterns (files downloaded, pages viewed, URLs clicked, access frequency and recency)

## Financial & Aid Data

- **Ellucian Banner Financial Aid** -- aid status changes (award adjustments, new awards, cancelled awards, revision dates, revision reasons), unmet need (cost of attendance minus EFC minus aid awarded, gap analysis by aid type), balance holds (outstanding balance amount, hold type, payment plan enrollment, hold impact on registration)
- **Student account / bursar systems** -- outstanding balances (current amount due, past-due amount, aging buckets: 30/60/90/120 days), payment plan status (plan type, installment amounts, payments made, payments remaining, delinquency status), tuition holds (hold amount, hold reason, registration/transcript impact, release conditions)
- **FAFSA / ISIR data** -- aid eligibility changes (SAI changes from corrections/updates, verification outcomes, dependency status changes), verification status (selected, verified, rejected, conflicting information flags, documentation received/pending)

## Housing & Campus Engagement

- **StarRez** -- housing assignments (building, room number, room type, occupancy, meal plan, assignment dates, contract status), roommate conflicts (incident reports, room change requests, mediation records, resolution status), incident reports (type: noise, policy violation, maintenance, safety; date, location, reporting party, resolution), check-in/check-out activity (arrival date, departure date, no-show flags, early departure indicators, break housing requests)
- **Symplicity / Presence** -- co-curricular involvement (organizations joined, leadership positions, involvement hours logged, organization type/category), event attendance (events attended, event types, attendance frequency, event categories), organization membership (active memberships, membership duration, participation level, co-curricular transcript data)
- **Campus card systems (CBORD, Transact)** -- meal plan usage (swipes used, swipes remaining, dining dollar balance, usage frequency, peak usage times, missed meal patterns), building access frequency (swipe-in data by building, time of day, day of week, irregular access patterns), library visits (entry/exit timestamps, visit frequency, duration, after-hours usage)

## Wellness & Support Services

- **Titanium Schedule / Medicat** -- counseling center appointments (aggregate appointment counts, show/no-show rates, wait times, referral sources; de-identified), health center visits (visit counts, appointment types, referral patterns; aggregate/de-identified), utilization trends (demand patterns, peak periods, capacity metrics)
- **Dean of Students case management** -- conduct cases (case type, severity, sanctions, outcomes, recidivism indicators), emergency aid requests (request type, amount, approval status, disbursement date, student demographics), crisis interventions (intervention type, referral source, services connected, follow-up plan)
- **CARE / BIT team records** -- behavioral intervention referrals (referral source, concern type: academic, behavioral, emotional, threat; date, assigned team member), threat assessment notes (risk level, recommended actions, monitoring plan, case status), case tracking (open/closed, interventions applied, external referrals made)

## Analytics & Reporting

- **Tableau / Power BI** -- retention dashboards (first-to-second year retention rate, term-to-term persistence rate, by cohort/demographics/program/entry type), cohort tracking (entry cohort size, retained, graduated, transferred, stopped out by term; disaggregated by demographic variables), DFW analysis (courses with highest DFW rates, DFW rates by section/instructor/term/student demographic, gateway course DFW rates, impact on retention), equity gap monitoring (retention/graduation gaps by race/ethnicity, gender, Pell status, first-generation status, gap trend over time)
- **IPEDS** -- institutional retention and graduation rate benchmarks (150% time graduation rate, retention rate, by gender, race/ethnicity, Pell status), peer institution comparison groups, Outcome Measures data (8-year outcomes by enrollment intensity and Pell status)
- **National Student Clearinghouse** -- stop-out and transfer-out tracking (subsequent enrollment institution, enrollment dates, program level, degree earned), re-enrollment monitoring (returning students after stop-out, time away, re-enrollment term, program), prior enrollment history for transfer students, completion rates for transfer-out students
