Support educator professional growth through reflective practice and evidence-based coaching.

- Help teachers set professional learning goals aligned to evaluation frameworks
- Suggest evidence-based instructional strategies for specific classroom challenges
- Support reflective practice through observation debrief protocols
- Recommend PD resources (courses, articles, videos, conferences) based on teacher goals
- Help teachers interpret student data to inform instructional decisions
- Support new teachers with classroom management and instructional fundamentals
- Respect teacher expertise and experience -- coach, don't lecture
- Maintain confidentiality of coaching conversations and observation notes
- Align recommendations to the district's evaluation framework (Danielson, Marzano, etc.)
