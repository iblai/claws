# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for K-12 special education support.

## Special Education Management Systems

- **Frontline Special Education** -- IEP and compliance management
  - **IEP data**: student_id, disability_category, eligibility_date, annual_review_date, reevaluation_date
  - **Goals**: goal_area, measurable_goal_text, baseline, target, progress_data, goal_status
  - **Services**: service_type (specialized_instruction, speech, OT, PT, counseling), frequency, duration, location, provider
  - **Accommodations**: accommodation_description, setting, frequency, implementation_notes

- **SEIS (Special Education Information System)** -- California-focused IEP system
  - **IEP records**: meeting_type, meeting_date, team_members, parent_participation, consent_status
  - **Compliance**: timeline_tracking, procedural_safeguards, prior_written_notice, manifestation_determinations
  - **Transition**: transition_goals, agency_linkages, post_secondary_outcomes

- **GoalBook** -- standards-aligned IEP goal bank and toolkit
  - **Goals**: goal_templates, aligned_standards, UDL_strategies, scaffolded_objectives
  - **Toolkits**: instructional_strategies, accommodation_suggestions, progress_monitoring_tools

- **Embrace (IEP management)** -- IEP authoring and compliance
  - **Documents**: IEP_draft, amendments, prior_written_notice, evaluation_reports, BIP
  - **Timelines**: annual_review_due, triennial_due, consent_dates, meeting_schedule

## Assessment & Progress Monitoring

- **NWEA MAP** -- academic growth data for special education students
  - **Accommodated testing**: accommodation_codes, standard_vs_accommodated_administration
  - **Growth data**: RIT_scores_over_time, growth_relative_to_peers, instructional_level

- **AIMSweb (Pearson)** -- progress monitoring and benchmarking
  - **CBM data**: probe_type (R-CBM, M-CBM, spelling), score, date, percentile, rate_of_improvement
  - **Benchmarks**: fall/winter/spring scores, risk_category, growth_trajectory

- **DIBELS (University of Oregon)** -- early literacy assessment
  - **Measures**: letter_naming_fluency, phoneme_segmentation, nonsense_word_fluency, oral_reading_fluency
  - **Risk levels**: at_risk, some_risk, low_risk, benchmark_status

- **Behavior tracking tools (LiveSchool, PBIS Rewards)** -- behavioral data
  - **Incidents**: behavior_type, antecedent, consequence, frequency, duration, intensity
  - **FBA data**: target_behavior, function_of_behavior, replacement_behavior, intervention_plan

## Compliance & Legal

- **IDEA (Individuals with Disabilities Education Act)** -- federal special education law
  - **Requirements**: child_find, FAPE, LRE, procedural_safeguards, parent_rights, due_process

- **Section 504 (Rehabilitation Act)** -- disability accommodations
  - **504 plans**: disability_description, major_life_activity_impacted, accommodations, responsible_staff

- **State special education regulations** -- state-specific requirements
  - **Timelines**: referral_to_evaluation (days), evaluation_to_IEP (days), annual_review, triennial
