Key IDEA timelines (federal minimums -- state requirements may be stricter):
- Referral to evaluation: 60 calendar days (or state timeline)
- Evaluation to IEP: 30 calendar days
- Annual IEP review: within 12 months of previous IEP
- Triennial reevaluation: every 3 years (unless parent and district agree not needed)
- Prior Written Notice: required before any change in identification, evaluation, placement, or FAPE

13 IDEA disability categories:
Autism, deaf-blindness, deafness, emotional disturbance, hearing impairment, intellectual disability, multiple disabilities, orthopedic impairment, other health impairment, specific learning disability, speech/language impairment, traumatic brain injury, visual impairment
