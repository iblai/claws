# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for K-12 student research support.

## Student-Safe Research Databases

- **Gale In Context (Elementary / Middle School / High School)** -- leveled research databases
  - **Content**: encyclopedia_articles, magazine_articles, academic_journals, multimedia, primary_sources
  - **Metadata**: reading_level, lexile, topic, content_type, citation_info, last_updated

- **EBSCO (Explora / Research Starters)** -- student-friendly database interface
  - **Content**: articles, essays, primary_sources, images, topic_overviews
  - **Features**: reading_level_indicator, citation_export, topic_tree_browsing

- **Britannica School** -- leveled encyclopedia (elementary/middle/high)
  - **Articles**: topic, reading_level (3 levels), related_articles, media, citations
  - **Features**: text-to-speech, translation, read_aloud, citation_builder

- **World Book Online** -- encyclopedia with student editions
  - **Content**: articles, primary_sources, videos, maps, timelines
  - **Levels**: Kids, Student, Advanced, Timelines, eBooks

## Library Systems

- **Follett Destiny** -- school library management system
  - **Catalog**: title, author, ISBN, call_number, subject_headings, lexile_level, availability
  - **Circulation**: check_out_date, due_date, renewals, holds, overdue_status
  - **Digital resources**: ebook_collection, database_links, curated_resource_lists

- **Alexandria** -- library automation system
  - **Catalog**: bibliographic_records, copy_information, patron_data, circulation_history
  - **Reports**: collection_analysis, usage_statistics, popular_titles

## Citation & Plagiarism Tools

- **EasyBib / Citation Machine** -- citation generators
  - **Formats**: MLA, APA, Chicago, Harvard
  - **Fields**: author, title, publication, date, URL, access_date, DOI

- **NoodleTools** -- research management for students
  - **Features**: notecards, outlines, citations, source_evaluation, collaboration
  - **Integration**: Google Docs export, LMS integration, teacher_review

## Open Educational Resources

- **Library of Congress (loc.gov)** -- primary source collections
  - **Collections**: digital_images, manuscripts, maps, audio, video, newspaper_archives
  - **Metadata**: date, creator, subject, format, rights_statement

- **National Archives (archives.gov)** -- government documents and records
  - **Resources**: documents, photographs, teaching_activities, document_analysis_worksheets

- **NASA.gov / NOAA / USGS** -- science data and educational resources
  - **Data**: satellite_imagery, climate_data, geological_data, space_mission_data, educational_activities
