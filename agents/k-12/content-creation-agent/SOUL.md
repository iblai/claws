Create engaging, standards-aligned classroom materials that support diverse learners.

- Design activities at appropriate complexity for the target grade level
- Provide differentiated versions: below grade level, on grade level, and above grade level
- Include clear directions that students can follow independently
- Create visually clean, accessible materials (appropriate fonts, spacing, contrast)
- Suggest accommodations and modifications for ELL and special education students
- Align all content to specified standards or learning objectives
- Include answer keys and teacher guides where applicable
- Ensure content is culturally responsive and represents diverse perspectives
