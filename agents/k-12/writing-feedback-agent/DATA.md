# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for K-12 writing feedback workflows.

## Writing & Literacy Platforms

- **Turnitin (with Feedback Studio)** -- plagiarism detection and writing feedback
  - **Submissions**: student_text, similarity_score, source_matches, feedback_comments, rubric_scores
  - **Revision history**: draft_versions, revision_timestamps, feedback_incorporation

- **NoRedInk** -- grammar and writing practice platform
  - **Student data**: skill_mastery (by grammar concept), practice_attempts, error_patterns
  - **Assignments**: topic, skill_focus, student_responses, auto_graded_scores

- **Writable (Houghton Mifflin Harcourt)** -- writing instruction platform
  - **Assignments**: prompt, genre, rubric, student_drafts, peer_review_comments
  - **Analytics**: class_writing_trends, common_errors, growth_by_trait

- **Google Docs (via Classroom)** -- collaborative writing
  - **Documents**: student_text, revision_history, comment_threads, suggestion_mode_edits
  - **Activity**: edit_timestamps, collaborator_contributions, word_count_changes

## Writing Standards & Rubrics

- **6+1 Traits of Writing** -- writing assessment framework
  - **Traits**: ideas, organization, voice, word_choice, sentence_fluency, conventions, presentation
  - **Rubric levels**: beginning (1), emerging (2), developing (3), proficient (4), strong (5)

- **Common Core Writing Standards** -- grade-level writing expectations
  - **Types**: argumentative, informative/explanatory, narrative
  - **Standards**: grade_level, text_type, standard_code, description, anchor_standard

- **AP / IB rubrics** -- advanced writing assessment
  - **Criteria**: thesis, evidence, analysis, sophistication, organization, conventions

## LMS & Gradebook

- **Canvas / Schoology / Google Classroom** -- assignment management
  - **Submissions**: student_id, assignment_id, submitted_text, draft_number, timestamp
  - **Rubric scores**: criterion, score, feedback_comment
  - **Peer review**: reviewer_id, review_text, rubric_scores

## Reading Level & Lexile Data

- **Lexile Framework** -- text complexity measurement
  - **Student reading level**: lexile_measure, grade_equivalent, confidence_band
- **Flesch-Kincaid** -- readability scoring
  - **Metrics**: reading_ease_score, grade_level, word_count, sentence_count, syllable_count
