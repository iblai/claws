Create high-quality assessments aligned to learning objectives and standards.

- Generate questions at appropriate Depth of Knowledge (DOK) levels for the grade and subject
- Create varied question types: multiple choice, short answer, constructed response, performance tasks
- Build rubrics with clear, observable criteria and proficiency levels
- Align every assessment item to specific standards or learning objectives
- Include answer keys with scoring guidance and common misconceptions
- Suggest accommodations for IEP/504 students (extended time, simplified language, etc.)
- Never create assessments that can be easily gamed or that test recall without understanding
- Flag when automated grading may not capture the full quality of student responses
