# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for K-12 curriculum alignment.

## Curriculum Mapping & Management

- **Atlas (Faria Education Group)** -- curriculum mapping platform
  - **Maps**: unit_name, grade, subject, standards_addressed, essential_questions, enduring_understandings
  - **Alignment**: standard_to_unit mapping, vertical_alignment_views, cross-curricular_connections
  - **Reports**: standards_coverage (taught/assessed/gap), pacing_adherence, revision_history

- **Chalk** -- curriculum planning and management
  - **Plans**: unit_plans, lesson_plans, standards_alignment, pacing_calendar
  - **Coverage**: standards_taught, standards_assessed, coverage_percentage_by_domain

- **Eduplanet21** -- UbD (Understanding by Design) curriculum platform
  - **Units**: stage1 (desired_results), stage2 (evidence), stage3 (learning_plan), transfer_goals
  - **Alignment**: standards, performance_tasks, rubrics, essential_questions

## Standards Databases

- **CASE Network (Competency and Academic Standards Exchange)** -- machine-readable standards
  - **Standards**: framework_id, standard_id, full_statement, grade_level, parent_standard, education_level
  - **Relationships**: is_child_of, is_related_to, precedes, replaced_by

- **Academic Benchmarks (Instructure)** -- standards alignment service
  - **Standards**: authority (state/national), subject, grade, standard_code, standard_text
  - **Crosswalks**: source_standard → target_standard, alignment_strength

- **Achieve the Core** -- Common Core alignment tools
  - **Shifts**: focus, coherence, rigor (for math); complexity, evidence, knowledge (for ELA)
  - **Evaluation criteria**: IMET (Instructional Materials Evaluation Tool) rubric

## Textbook & Resource Evaluation

- **EdReports** -- independent curriculum reviews
  - **Reviews**: publisher, title, grade, subject, gateway_scores, alignment_rating, usability_rating
  - **Criteria**: text_quality_and_complexity, building_knowledge, usability, teacher_support

- **Louisiana Believes Curriculum Reviews** -- state-reviewed materials
  - **Ratings**: tier_rating (1/2/3), overall_score, indicator_scores, reviewer_notes

## Learning Standards

- **Common Core (CCSS)** -- math and ELA K-12
  - **Structure**: domain → cluster → standard, mathematical_practices, anchor_standards
- **NGSS** -- science K-12
  - **Structure**: disciplinary_core_idea → performance_expectation, three_dimensions
- **C3 Framework** -- social studies
  - **Dimensions**: developing_questions, applying_tools, evaluating_sources, communicating_conclusions
- **National Core Arts Standards** -- arts education
  - **Processes**: creating, performing/presenting/producing, responding, connecting
- **SHAPE America** -- physical education and health
  - **Standards**: motor_skills, movement_concepts, physical_activity, fitness, responsible_behavior
