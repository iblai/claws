# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for K-12 student safety monitoring.

## Student Safety Platforms

- **Gaggle** -- student safety management for email and cloud content
  - **Alerts**: alert_type (self-harm, bullying, violence, drugs, nudity), severity, student_id, content_snippet, source_platform
  - **Review queue**: flagged_content, context, reviewer_notes, action_taken, escalation_status
  - **Trends**: alert_volume_by_type, school_comparison, time_of_day_patterns

- **Bark for Schools** -- social media and device monitoring
  - **Alerts**: category (cyberbullying, depression, suicidal_ideation, violence, sexual_content), severity_level
  - **Monitored platforms**: email, Drive, Docs, chat, social_media_mentions
  - **Reports**: daily_alert_summary, trend_analysis, response_time_metrics

- **GoGuardian** -- classroom management and web filtering
  - **Browsing**: URLs_visited, time_on_site, blocked_attempts, flagged_searches
  - **Beacon (self-harm detection)**: alert_type, trigger_content, student_id, notification_recipients
  - **Classroom sessions**: active_tabs, screen_thumbnails, chat_messages, teacher_actions

- **Securly** -- web filtering and student wellness
  - **Filter data**: allowed/blocked URLs, category, override_requests, policy_violations
  - **Wellness signals**: sentiment_analysis, concerning_search_terms, behavioral_patterns
  - **Tip line**: anonymous_tips, category, urgency_level, follow_up_status

## Behavioral & Incident Tracking

- **PBIS Rewards** -- positive behavioral interventions and supports
  - **Data**: student_points, behavior_type (positive/negative), location, time, staff_reporter
  - **Trends**: school_wide_behavior_data, tier_classifications, intervention_effectiveness

- **LiveSchool** -- behavior tracking and culture management
  - **Records**: behavior_event, points, category, teacher, period, student_response
  - **Analytics**: daily/weekly trends, behavior_by_location, referral_patterns

- **SWIS (School-Wide Information System)** -- office discipline referral tracking
  - **Referrals**: problem_behavior, location, time, others_involved, administrative_decision
  - **Reports**: referrals_per_day_per_month, by_behavior, by_location, by_time, by_student

## Wellness & SEL

- **Panorama Education** -- social-emotional learning surveys and analytics
  - **Survey data**: SEL_competency_scores (self-management, social_awareness, growth_mindset, self-efficacy)
  - **Equity metrics**: perception_gaps_by_subgroup, belonging_index, teacher_student_relationship_scores

- **CASEL framework** -- SEL competency reference
  - **Competencies**: self-awareness, self-management, social_awareness, relationship_skills, responsible_decision_making
