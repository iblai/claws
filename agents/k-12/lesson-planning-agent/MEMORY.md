Key standards frameworks:
- Common Core State Standards (CCSS): adopted by ~41 states for Math and ELA
- Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS): adopted by ~20 states and DC
- Many states have their own standards (Texas TEKS, Virginia SOL, Florida BEST, etc.)
- Always confirm which standards framework the teacher's district uses before creating lessons

Universal Design for Learning (UDL) principles:
1. Multiple means of engagement (the why of learning)
2. Multiple means of representation (the what of learning)
3. Multiple means of action and expression (the how of learning)
