# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for small business social media management.

## Social Media Platforms

- **Instagram Business** -- visual content platform
  - **Posts**: image/video/carousel/reel, caption, hashtags, location, tagged_accounts, publish_date
  - **Insights**: reach, impressions, engagement (likes/comments/saves/shares), profile_visits, website_clicks
  - **Audience**: follower_count, demographics (age/gender/location), active_hours, growth_rate
  - **Stories**: views, replies, exits, taps_forward/back, sticker_interactions

- **Facebook Business Page** -- community and advertising
  - **Posts**: text, media, link, publish_date, boost_status, organic_reach, paid_reach
  - **Insights**: page_likes, post_reach, engagement_rate, video_views, click-through_rate
  - **Messages**: customer_inquiries, response_time, automated_responses, conversation_threads
  - **Events**: event_name, date, RSVPs (going/interested), reach, ticket_link

- **Google Business Profile** -- local search and maps
  - **Posts**: update_type (offer/event/product/update), text, image, CTA, views, clicks
  - **Performance**: search_impressions, map_views, calls, direction_requests, website_visits, messages
  - **Photos**: owner_photos, customer_photos, views_by_photo, photo_count_vs_competitors

- **TikTok Business** -- short-form video
  - **Videos**: caption, hashtags, sounds, duration, views, likes, comments, shares, saves
  - **Analytics**: follower_growth, video_views_trend, audience_demographics, traffic_sources
  - **Trending**: trending_sounds, trending_hashtags, trending_formats

- **LinkedIn Company Page** -- B2B and professional networking
  - **Posts**: text, media, document, publish_date, impressions, clicks, engagement_rate
  - **Followers**: count, demographics (industry/company_size/function/seniority), growth
  - **Analytics**: unique_visitors, page_views, custom_button_clicks, competitor_comparison

## Social Media Management Tools

- **Hootsuite** -- social scheduling and analytics
  - **Scheduled posts**: platform, content, media, publish_time, status, approval_state
  - **Analytics**: engagement_by_platform, best_performing_posts, audience_growth, response_time

- **Buffer** -- simple social scheduling
  - **Queue**: post_content, platform, scheduled_time, published_status, engagement_metrics
  - **Analytics**: reach, engagement, clicks, top_posts, best_posting_times

- **Later** -- visual-first social planning
  - **Content calendar**: post, media_preview, scheduled_date, platform, caption, first_comment (hashtags)
  - **Linkin.bio**: clicks, page_views, top_links, revenue_attribution

- **Canva** -- design platform
  - **Designs**: template_used, dimensions (platform-specific), brand_colors, elements, export_format
  - **Brand kit**: colors, fonts, logos, templates, brand_guidelines
