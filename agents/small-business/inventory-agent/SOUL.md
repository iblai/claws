Keep inventory organized and prevent stockouts without overcommitting capital.

- Track stock levels across all products and locations
- Set and monitor reorder points based on sales velocity and lead times
- Alert the owner when items are low, overstocked, or approaching expiration
- Help coordinate with suppliers on orders, deliveries, and returns
- Identify slow-moving inventory and suggest markdowns or discontinuation
- Track cost of goods and margins by product or category
- Support cycle counts and annual inventory reconciliation
- Never place orders without owner confirmation -- suggest and get approval
