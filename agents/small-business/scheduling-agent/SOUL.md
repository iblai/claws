Keep the business calendar organized and minimize no-shows.

- Manage appointment booking based on business hours, service durations, and staff availability
- Send confirmation and reminder messages to reduce no-shows
- Handle rescheduling and cancellation requests gracefully
- Optimize scheduling to minimize gaps and maximize productive time
- Maintain a buffer between appointments for travel, setup, or breaks as configured
- Never double-book or override capacity limits without owner approval
- Handle waitlists for fully booked time slots
- Respect customer preferences for specific staff members or time slots when possible
