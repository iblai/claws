# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for small business sales.

## CRM & Pipeline

- **HubSpot CRM (free)** -- contact and deal management
  - **Contacts**: name, email, phone, company, source, lifecycle_stage, owner, custom_properties
  - **Deals**: deal_name, amount, stage (appointment/proposal/negotiation/won/lost), close_date, probability
  - **Activities**: emails_sent, calls_made, meetings_booked, notes, task_reminders
  - **Reports**: deal_forecast, pipeline_velocity, conversion_rates_by_stage, revenue_by_source

- **Salesforce Essentials** -- small business CRM
  - **Leads**: name, company, source, status, score, conversion_date
  - **Opportunities**: name, amount, stage, expected_close, next_steps, competitors
  - **Activities**: task_type, due_date, priority, related_contact, completed_status

- **Pipedrive** -- sales-focused CRM for small teams
  - **Deals**: title, value, stage, probability, expected_close, rotting_days, activities_count
  - **Pipeline**: stage_conversion_rates, average_deal_size, average_sales_cycle, win_rate

- **Zoho CRM** -- affordable CRM suite
  - **Modules**: leads, contacts, accounts, deals, activities, products, quotes
  - **Analytics**: lead_source_ROI, deal_stage_duration, sales_by_owner, forecasting

## Proposal & Quoting

- **PandaDoc** -- proposals and e-signatures
  - **Documents**: template_name, status (draft/sent/viewed/signed), view_count, time_spent, signer_info
  - **Quotes**: line_items, pricing, discounts, tax, total, expiration_date

- **HoneyBook** -- client management for service businesses
  - **Projects**: client_name, service_type, project_value, status, invoices, contracts, files
  - **Pipeline**: inquiry_date, proposal_sent, contract_signed, retainer_paid, project_complete

- **Jobber** -- quoting and invoicing for field services
  - **Quotes**: client, line_items, total, status (draft/sent/approved/converted), job_number
  - **Follow-ups**: quote_age, last_viewed, follow_up_date, conversion_status

## Lead Generation

- **Google Ads** -- paid search leads
  - **Campaigns**: campaign_name, clicks, impressions, cost, conversions, cost_per_conversion, keywords
  - **Leads**: form_submissions, call_clicks, location_actions, conversion_source

- **Facebook / Instagram Ads** -- social media leads
  - **Leads**: lead_form_submissions, contact_info, ad_source, cost_per_lead, audience_segment

- **LinkedIn** -- B2B networking and leads
  - **Connections**: name, title, company, industry, shared_connections, messages
