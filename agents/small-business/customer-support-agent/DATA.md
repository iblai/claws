# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for small business customer support.

## Review & Reputation Platforms

- **Google Business Profile** -- business listing and reviews
  - **Reviews**: reviewer_name, star_rating, review_text, date, owner_response, response_date
  - **Insights**: search_queries, views (maps/search), actions (calls/directions/website), photo_views
  - **Q&A**: question_text, answer_text, upvotes

- **Yelp for Business** -- review platform
  - **Reviews**: star_rating, review_text, date, photos, useful/funny/cool_votes, owner_response
  - **Business info**: hours, categories, attributes, claimed_status
  - **Analytics**: page_views, customer_leads, revenue_estimates

- **Facebook Business Page** -- social media reviews and messaging
  - **Reviews**: recommendation (yes/no), review_text, date, commenter
  - **Messages**: customer_message, response_time, conversation_thread, resolved_status
  - **Insights**: page_reach, engagement, response_rate, response_time_average

## CRM & Customer Management

- **HubSpot CRM (free)** -- contact and deal management
  - **Contacts**: name, email, phone, company, lifecycle_stage, last_contacted, notes
  - **Deals**: deal_name, amount, stage, close_date, associated_contacts
  - **Activity**: emails, calls, meetings, tasks, notes, timeline

- **Zoho CRM** -- small business CRM
  - **Contacts**: name, email, phone, source, lead_status, account, last_activity
  - **Tickets**: subject, status, priority, channel, response_time, resolution_time

- **Freshdesk** -- help desk for small teams
  - **Tickets**: subject, description, priority, status, assignee, tags, SLA_status
  - **Canned responses**: response_name, body, category, usage_count
  - **Reports**: tickets_by_channel, resolution_time, satisfaction_score, busiest_hours

## Communication

- **Gmail / Google Workspace** -- email communication
  - **Emails**: sender, recipient, subject, body, date, thread, labels, attachments

- **Twilio / SimpleTexting** -- SMS and messaging
  - **Messages**: phone_number, message_text, direction (inbound/outbound), timestamp, delivery_status

- **Intercom / Drift / LiveChat** -- website chat
  - **Conversations**: visitor_info, message_thread, page_visited, response_time, resolution
