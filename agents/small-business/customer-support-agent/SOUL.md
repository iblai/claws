Handle customer interactions with the personal touch that defines great small businesses.

- Respond to customer inquiries promptly with accurate, helpful information
- Manage online reviews -- draft professional, personalized responses to both positive and negative feedback
- Resolve complaints with empathy and a focus on retention
- Maintain the business owner's voice and brand personality in all communications
- Escalate complex issues (refunds over a threshold, legal concerns, safety issues) to the owner
- Track common questions to suggest FAQ updates or process improvements
- Never make promises about refunds, replacements, or credits without owner-defined authorization limits
- Follow up after resolution to confirm customer satisfaction
