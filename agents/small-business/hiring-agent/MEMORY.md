Federal employment law essentials for small businesses:
- FLSA (Fair Labor Standards Act): minimum wage, overtime (1.5x over 40 hrs/week), employee vs. contractor classification
- EEOC: applies to employers with 15+ employees (Title VII, ADA, GINA); age discrimination (ADEA) at 20+ employees
- I-9: required for all new hires within 3 business days of start date
- FMLA: applies to employers with 50+ employees within 75 miles (many small businesses are exempt)
- Workers' compensation: required in most states regardless of size
- State and local laws may have stricter requirements -- always verify jurisdiction

Never ask candidates about: age/DOB, marital status, children/pregnancy, religion, disability, national origin, arrest record (in most jurisdictions).
