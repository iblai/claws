# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for small business hiring.

## Job Boards & Applicant Tracking

- **Indeed** -- job posting and applicant management
  - **Postings**: job_title, description, location, salary_range, job_type (full/part/contract), posting_date, status
  - **Applicants**: name, email, phone, resume, cover_letter, apply_date, source, screening_questions_responses
  - **Analytics**: views, applies, apply_rate, cost_per_application, sponsored_vs_organic

- **LinkedIn Jobs** -- professional network job posting
  - **Postings**: title, description, skills_required, experience_level, salary_range, remote/hybrid/onsite
  - **Applicants**: profile_link, headline, experience_summary, skills_match, easy_apply_data
  - **Insights**: applicant_demographics, competitor_job_postings, salary_benchmarks

- **ZipRecruiter** -- AI-powered job matching
  - **Candidates**: name, resume, match_score, source, status, recruiter_notes
  - **Analytics**: views, applies, quality_candidates, source_effectiveness

## Small Business ATS

- **Homebase** -- scheduling and hiring for hourly workers
  - **Applicants**: name, contact, availability, position_applied, source, status, interview_notes
  - **Screening**: custom_questions, responses, knockout_criteria

- **JazzHR** -- affordable ATS for small businesses
  - **Candidates**: name, resume, stage (applied/screened/interviewed/offered/hired), evaluations, source
  - **Pipeline**: stage_counts, time_in_stage, conversion_rates, offer_acceptance_rate

- **Breezy HR** -- visual hiring pipeline
  - **Candidates**: profile, resume, cover_letter, stage, scorecards, team_feedback, tags
  - **Automation**: stage_triggers, auto_reject_criteria, interview_scheduling

## Payroll & Onboarding

- **Gusto** -- payroll, benefits, HR for small businesses
  - **Employees**: name, position, hire_date, salary/hourly_rate, pay_schedule, tax_info, benefits_elections
  - **Onboarding**: offer_letter_status, I-9, W-4, direct_deposit, handbook_acknowledgment

- **Homebase** -- time tracking and team management
  - **Team**: employee_name, role, availability, hire_date, hourly_rate, overtime_status

## Background & Verification

- **Checkr** -- background checks
  - **Reports**: status (pending/complete/consider/clear), criminal_records, driving_records, education_verification
  - **Packages**: basic/standard/comprehensive, turnaround_time, county_searches

## Compliance Reference

- **EEOC guidelines** -- equal employment opportunity
  - **Protected classes**: race, color, religion, sex, national_origin, age, disability, genetic_information
  - **Requirements**: posting_requirements, record_keeping, anti-retaliation
