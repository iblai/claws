# Data Sources

Systems and platforms commonly accessed for small business website management.

## Content Management Systems (CMS)

- **WordPress** -- most popular website platform
  - **Pages**: title, slug, content, status (draft/published/scheduled), author, template, SEO_meta
  - **Posts**: title, content, categories, tags, featured_image, publish_date, comments
  - **Plugins**: Yoast/RankMath SEO (focus_keyword, readability_score, SEO_score, meta_description)
  - **Media**: filename, alt_text, dimensions, file_size, upload_date

- **Squarespace** -- visual website builder
  - **Pages**: title, URL_slug, content, SEO_title, SEO_description, social_image
  - **Blog**: posts, categories, tags, comments, featured_image, excerpt
  - **Analytics**: unique_visitors, page_views, traffic_sources, popular_content, form_submissions

- **Wix** -- website builder with business tools
  - **Pages**: page_name, URL, content, SEO_settings, mobile_optimization
  - **Blog**: posts, categories, SEO_data, social_sharing_stats
  - **Ascend**: forms, chat, email_marketing, invoices integrated in platform

- **Shopify** -- e-commerce website
  - **Pages**: title, content, template, URL_handle, SEO_title, SEO_description
  - **Products**: title, description, images, variants, price, SEO_fields, availability
  - **Blog**: articles, tags, author, publish_date, SEO_fields

## Analytics & Search

- **Google Analytics 4** -- website traffic and behavior
  - **Traffic**: sessions, users, new_vs_returning, source/medium, campaign, landing_page
  - **Behavior**: page_views, average_engagement_time, bounce_rate, scroll_depth, events
  - **Conversions**: goals (form_submit/call_click/purchase), conversion_rate, attribution_path
  - **Demographics**: country, city, device, browser, language, age_range, interests

- **Google Search Console** -- search performance
  - **Queries**: search_term, impressions, clicks, CTR, average_position, page
  - **Indexing**: indexed_pages, crawl_errors, sitemap_status, mobile_usability_issues
  - **Core Web Vitals**: LCP, FID/INP, CLS, mobile_vs_desktop, URL-level_scores

- **Google Business Profile** -- local search presence
  - **Listing**: business_name, address, phone, hours, categories, attributes, description, photos
  - **Performance**: search_impressions, map_views, calls, direction_requests, website_clicks, message_queries
  - **Reviews**: rating, review_text, response, date, reviewer_name

## SEO Tools

- **SEMrush / Ahrefs / Moz** -- SEO research and monitoring
  - **Keywords**: keyword, search_volume, difficulty, current_position, SERP_features, CPC
  - **Backlinks**: referring_domain, anchor_text, domain_authority, follow/nofollow, first_seen
  - **Site audit**: errors (broken_links, missing_alt_text, duplicate_content), warnings, passed_checks

- **Yoast SEO / RankMath (WordPress)** -- on-page SEO
  - **Analysis**: focus_keyword, SEO_score, readability_score, meta_title, meta_description, schema_markup
  - **Sitemap**: pages_in_sitemap, last_modified, excluded_pages
