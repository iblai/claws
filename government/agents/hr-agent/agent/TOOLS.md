Available integrations for government human resources:

- **Workday Government / PeopleSoft HRMS / NFC (National Finance Center)** — query employee position and personnel data, leave balances, pay information, and performance plan status; initiate personnel action request (PAR) workflows
- **USA Staffing / USAJOBS** — query vacancy announcements, applicant roster, certificate of eligibles, and hiring action status
- **Benefits management (Employee Express / BENEFEDS / OPM)** — check FEHB, FEGLI, FEDVIP enrollment status; retrieve open season dates and plan comparison tools; display TSP contribution elections and matching information
- **Leave management system** — query leave balances (annual, sick, FMLA, LWOP, comp time, credit hours); submit and track leave requests; retrieve supervisor approval queue
- **Performance management system** — view current performance plan, rating history, appraisal period dates, and critical element descriptions
- **EEO / Employee Relations case tracking** — retrieve process guides and timelines for EEO complaints, grievance procedures, and adverse action appeals (read-only; case details require authorized access)
- **OPM policy portal** — retrieve federal HR policy documents, operating manuals (FEHB Handbook, CSRS/FERS Handbook), pay tables, and benefits calculator tools

## Data Sources

Systems and platforms for government human resources administration.

### Personnel & Payroll

- **Workday Government / NFC** — position data (position number, title, series, grade, pay plan, pay band, organization, duty station, FTE, position type: permanent/term/temporary, competitive/excepted service), personnel actions (Nature of Action code, effective date, from/to pay plan/series/grade, SF-50 data), pay (basic pay, locality pay, special rate, total compensation, tax withholdings, TSP deductions, FEHB premium, net pay), personnel records (SF-50 history, performance ratings, awards, training completions, clearance level)

### Staffing & Hiring

- **USA Staffing** — vacancy announcements (announcement number, position title, series, grade, pay scale, location, open/close dates, appointment type, status), certificates (certificate number, eligible list, selection status, veterans preference application), applicant actions (name, application date, preference, status: referred/not referred/selected/not selected)

### Benefits

- **Employee Express / BENEFEDS** — FEHB enrollment (plan name, plan code, premium: employee/government share, enrollment type: self/self+one/self+family, open season election, qualifying life event), FEGLI coverage (coverage amount, option type, premium), TSP account (contribution percentage, contribution type: traditional/Roth, fund allocation, loan balance), FEDVIP enrollment (dental/vision plan, premium, effective date)

### Leave

- **Leave management** — leave balances (annual leave: current balance, use-or-lose threshold, max carryover; sick leave: balance; FMLA: designated hours used/remaining; LWOP: cumulative hours; comp time: earned/used/balance; credit hours: earned/used/balance), leave requests (request ID, employee, leave type, start/end date, hours, status: pending/approved/denied, supervisor, reason for denial)

### Performance

- **ePerformance / USA Performance** — performance plans (plan ID, appraisal period, critical elements, performance standards, employee/supervisor signatures, plan status), ratings (rating date, element ratings, summary rating: outstanding/exceeds/fully successful/minimally satisfactory/unacceptable, rating official, reviewer)
